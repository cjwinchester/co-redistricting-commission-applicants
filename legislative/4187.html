Organization and Advocacy Experience

Since I moved to Colorado in Sept. 2015, I have not worked extensively as an organizer or advocate. However, I have experience with prioritizing community needs and listening to a variety of points of view and coming to a consensus: I was a team captain for 2 years on the swim team in undergrad, I completed a research project on wage theft in Denver in graduate school, and I have worked for over 7 years with youth in various capacities. And I try to stay involved in initiatives, campaigns, and daily work of a variety of organizations in Colorado from the Colorado Immigrant Rights Coalition to the DPS school board.

Statement

Quite honestly, I am making a concerted effort to become more involved in the governmental activities of our state. I loved my experience as an election judge, and I consider the representation of all Coloradans in federal and state legislature an incredibly important initiative in 2021, especially as it relates to communities of interest. Colorado has a variety of constituents who need to be fairly and equitably represented and resourced, and I hope to be a part of the commission that hears their needs and prioritizes them in the districting process.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Since I am not intimately involved in organizing or advocating on behalf of my political party, I consider myself to be more of a "Switzerland" political representative. I want to hear from the people who are going to be affected by legislative changes: what are their concerns and how do we effectively address those concerns as a commission? And I believe that ultimately we are not bringing any of our personal beliefs to the commission- this is a job that is intended to be impartial, and it is a necessity to hear from others and to use data to determine the best possible scenarios.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am comfortable synthesizing information from multiple sources in a concise brief/summary, and I have worked over the past ten years in school and in a variety of jobs to maintain a critical lens through which I view any problem, especially as it relates to social/economic/political issues. I am comfortable with both quantitative and qualitative analysis and consider both methodologies to be crucial for a full understanding of a problem and a potential solution.
