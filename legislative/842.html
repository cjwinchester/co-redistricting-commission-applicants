Statement

I believe that the intent of districting (redistricting), of drawing electoral district boundaries that are equitable, non-advantageous, and non-discriminatory; have failed over the years, resulting in targeted and specific gerrymandering, despite the Voting Rights Act. I hope to be part of a commission that will work to change this long held pattern of behavior and put districts back into alignment where every voters vote matters. There should not be an advantage one way or the other determined by which party has control at a given re-districting year. The districts should be drawn based on population and an equitable division between parties within a district.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe the structure of the commission, and the person(s) who lead it will go a long way to determine the success of the commission. Everyone should have an equal voice on the commission. Everyone should feel safe to voice their opinion without fear of retribution, ridicule, or judgement. The commission should be made up of like minded individuals who have the ultimate goal of making the best decisions possible when it comes to drawing districts. Working together towards this goal should be the aim of everyone who wants to join. Ensuring that all voices are heard at the table, that all points of view are considered, laying down a framework that allows for that would be the first thing I would advocate for. I served in the military for 8 years and was a registered Republican. I am married to a Democrat in public service. I see pros and cons to both parties, which is why I am unaffiliated. I don't believe either party is 'evil', nor do I believe either party has all the answers. I believe when it comes to redistricting, however, we need to work for the people, not the politicians. This process should be about fairness to the people of Colorado.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

In a prior life, before becoming an executive, I spent 20+ in Information Technology, culminating as a Chief Technology Officer. Logic, analysis, and problem solving have always been my strong suits. As a leader, I believe in putting the best people in positions to excel and that my job is merely to help them succeed. On a commission the same rule applies. Hopefully the best people are chosen, the leaders and members of the commission have the role of making sure everyone succeeds in their roles.
