Statement on Working with Consensus

My education in journalism has made me dedicated to truth and balance in all aspects of our political process. Personal therapy has helped me understand human tendencies towards aggression and blame when they are in challenging circumstances. Seemingly unresolvable arguments between dedicated, honest, well-meaning folks can often be boiled down to a disparity in information between two sides, which must be corrected firmly and compassionately. There can be no tolerance for dishonesty and self-serving behavior as it undermines all communication.

I have been driven by problem solving and proven my ability to learn analytical and logic skills.
