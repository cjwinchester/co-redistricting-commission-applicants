Statement

First, I would like to serve on the commission because I have a heart to serve. Second, there must be fairness and representation of all people in order to gain different perspectives to make better decisions and lastly I understand my civic responsibility. Also having more fair and equitable congressional and legislative maps will give Coloradans better and more accurate representation which is important moving forward as we make Colorado an even better place to serve and live.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I will promote consensus and contribute to the commission in a meaningful way amongst commissioners by acknowledging all perspectives and by staying true to my truths which is equity, justice and fairness for all people as well as leading with integrity and grit coupled with my data-driven mindset that will help lead to healthy decision making practices. Consensus building is meeting the interest of everyone and being in agreement with what will maximize opportunities for everyone.
