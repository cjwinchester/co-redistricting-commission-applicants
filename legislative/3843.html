I am very logically and math minded. This has served me well as a computer programer and now as a Professional Photo Manager. I am able to analyse a large project and break it up into smaller steps. For instance, in my job it is very common for someone to give me several bins of loose photos. At the end of an hour of discussing the photos and what she would like done with them we come up with a plan (or smaller projects) and prioritize them. Many times she will not have even known what she wanted and I am able to give other suggestions. I then take the photos, which initially usually involves organizing them in some way. I do an overview and somewhat of a deep dive into everything she has given me. If the first step is to organize, I decide at what level (decade, year) to do that. Should I take apart the old albums first. Should we get the outdated movies and slides converted. There is analysis, logic & reason that goes into each of those decisions based facts that the client has or has not given me.
