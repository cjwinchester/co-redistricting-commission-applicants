Background

Past Political Activity

I am an active Democrat. Over the past five years I have registered voters (2016), and worked as a Slack Text Team Leader (2020) for the Colorado Democrats. This involved texting voters in Colorado about the Presidential, state and county elections. The main responsibility as a Leader was to help the folks who were texting voters usingThruText as the vehicle for communication with voters and Slack for communicating with the leaders and the staff. The main responsibilities were to answer questions about the procedure and provide resources to help voters who had questions about the candidates and the issues. I worked remotely an average of 3 hours/day form August 19th through November 3rd. I also spent considerable time researching issues and candidates to prepare for my role as Text Team Leader.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I am a retired elementary school principal. I held administrative positions for 14 years as an assistant principal (7 years) and principal (7 years) in both Texas and Jefferson County Schools (CO). Those jobs required excellent listening, synthesizing, and conflict resolution skills. A major part of the job was to plan, compromise and reach consensus towards a common goal. I believe those skills would serve me well as a member of the Colorado Redistricting Commission. I also have a good sense of humor and have a daily meditation practice.

Statement

I have been very concerned about gerrymandering in our country. This system is designed with non-democratic goals in mind. I believe the voice of the people are central to a vibrant and honest democracy. I would be honored to work toward this end in Colorado.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As stated above, my experience as a principal is very relevant to this position. The education of children does not lay in neutral territory. Elementary education is one of the few fields in which everyone has past experience - either as a student, parent, teacher, or citizen. A principal is must respond to and work collaboratively with all of these constituents in order to be successful. I also think serving on a jury is relevant experience, My peers on a jury came with very different views and life experiences. I found it revealing and helpful to work with people outside of my usual bubble.
