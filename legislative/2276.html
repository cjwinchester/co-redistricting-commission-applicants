Statement

Having demographically representative districts is key to a republic. As we are not a direct democracy, we rely on the voice of groups of us to elect an appropriate representative, thus, the make-up of those groups must reflect the diversity in the group to ensure the selected representation adequately advocates. It is my duty to offer to serve to ensure the validity of this process.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I bring with me the experience of multiple rulemakings at the Colorado Air Quality Commission and the Colorado Oil and Gas Conservation Commission.
