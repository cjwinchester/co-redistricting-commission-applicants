Organization and Advocacy Experience

I have a long history of advocating for positions or groups in my professional career. I served on CDOT's I-70 PACT and was charged with identifying the preferred alignment (via consensus) for the Central 70 project. The PACT was made up of business members, local residents and community members and the process took well over a year to complete.

Statement

Representation is a key component of our democracy and the way to ensure this is to help create competitive districts.

Statement on Working with Consensus

When working with diverse groups with diverse views, its critical to be able to compromise and promote consensus. The way to achieve this is by keeping front and center the goal/mission of the project, not having an ego and having the ability to not be driven by personal and political preferences.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

The ability to be a critical thinker and analyze and make data driven decisions are skills I possess. Those skills are key as I complete my doctoral dissertation and key to performing my job of analyzing program interventions and their impacts to under resourced neighborhoods.
