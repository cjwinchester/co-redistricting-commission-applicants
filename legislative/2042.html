Background

Professional Background

My professional work in Colorado has involved advocating on behalf of children, youth, education, food security and more. From 2006-2008 I was Executive Director of the Colorado Alliance for Quality Teaching and successfully worked with state legislators to develop and get passed legislation to establish a unique teacher identifier for every Colorado teacher so that data could inform work to ensure quality teachers for ALL children. In Extension, my role is more educational by providing factual information on issues that affect the health, quality of life and well-being of Colorado citizens.

Past Political Activity

Campaigned in 2008 for Barack Obama; donated money at various times to Democratic Party, Evie Hudak campaign, Angelika Schroeder campaign for State Board of Education

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

ESP - Epsilon Sigma Phi, professional association of Extension educators; West Chamber Colorado; appointed to Colorado Quality Teachers Commission; currently appointed to CO Consumer Insurance Council; Health Ministry Team - Faith Lutheran Church; Jeffco Food Policy Council; Jeffco Family Leadership Training Institute Civic Design Team

Statement

I have been disturbed be gerrymandering of districts and believe it is critical that we have as fair a system as possible. This is even more important at this time of extreme differences in our county and I think my clear head, critical thinking, organizational abilities and commitment will allow me to make a difference on this commission.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Equity is important to me personally and I have always worked to build consensus. My professional work with people of different opinions and different backgrounds has given me many opportunities to build consensus and find ways for individuals to find common ground.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As a professional leader I have used logic and reason to come to many difficult decisions. Most recently, I led the staff through difficult conversations about required budget cuts due to Jeffco's TABOR limits. I was able to work through the issues, develop multiple scenarios and come up with a plan that is balanced and will limit disruption to the work. Simple decisions comparing various contractors or equipment through spreadsheets and analysis to determine the best solution are common in the roles I have held.
