Past Political Activity

Each year I donate small amounts to various candidates and political organizations. Each donation tends to be $50 - $100.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I am volunteer Treasurer of Grove at Mesa Meadows HOA. I also volunteer as a trainer and site coordinator for AARP Tax-Aide. I am volunteer Treasurer for Animal Evac Volunteers, a member of the Jefferson County Animal Rescue Team. I volunteer at The Action Center in Jefferson County doing data entry, working in the clothing bank, and helping at special events. I am a member of the League of Women Voters.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

As part of Animal Evac Volunteers, I participate in community outreach activities to help people understand how to plan for an emergency and how to request help to evacuate their animals. Also, I occasionally attend community events as a volunteer representing the Action Center. For AARP Tax-Aide, I help conduct training sessions before the tax season. I also manage the Standley Lake Library site for AARP Tax-Aide.

Statement

Our democracy requires fair voting districts. I think that the best way to accomplish that is to involve voters in developing those districts.

Statement on Working with Consensus

My goal as a commissioner would be to produce maps that provide the best opportunity for all voters to have their say in each election. I know that the various commissioners will have varying ideas about how to accomplish the goal, but I think that we will be able to agree that we want to produce the fairest maps possible. During the process, I would use open-ended questions and follow-ups and a respectful attitude to help the commission get to a solution that serves the voters.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My background is in business and supply chain management, so I am accustomed to analyzing information and considering alternatives. I know how to listen to varying perspectives before making a decision.
