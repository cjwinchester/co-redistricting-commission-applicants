Background

Past Political Activity

I have primarily been active by donating money. In 2004 I walked for John Kerry. Since 2008 I've been a contributor to federal election campaigns, including Obama's and Bennett's. Since 2015 I've been a contributor to ActBlue generally, and to Clinton in 2015-16, and Hickenlooper and Biden in 2019-2020. I may have also sent money to the Democrat legislative effort in 2016 or 2018 (I can't recall which year they had the big push).

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Board of Trustees Colorado Legal Aid Foundation; Board of Trustees, Colorado Water Trust; Co-chair Board of Advisors to the National Judicial College's Dividing the Waters Program; co-chair of the Year in Review, American Bar Association water law chapter

Organization and Advocacy Experience

Starting in 2005 I worked with Public Counsel of the Rockies, an advocacy group that worked on water issues related to coalbed methane development, resulting in a Colorado Supreme Court decision, Vance v. Wolfe. I don't have any experience with community organizing. Professionally my advocacy focuses on water rights matters for public and private clients.

Statement

I am a strong believer in the Commission system of redistricting, and want to be a part of setting its policies and priorities at the onset. I hope this can play a role in trying to provide voters with more representative districts and as a result, better government. I see this as a way to "give back" to the community in a big way.

Statement on Working with Consensus

In my practice--and in life for that matter--I have learned its better to identify what people can do rather than what they can't do. In that regard, in the midst of disagreements and dispute, identifying the path forward that works for most is best and I have a lot of experience doing just that. I'm a trial lawyer but most of the results I'm most proud of are the cases that settled--and this is something my clients would agree iwth as well. Finally, I helped raise four children and identifying ways to be fair, impartial, and also "sell" the idea of something are key to good parenting.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My day to day law practice requires me to apply analytical skills to all manner of problems, from large to small. I especially look for opportunities to develop creative solutions to difficult situations.
