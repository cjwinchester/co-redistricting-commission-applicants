Past Political Activity

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

The extent of my official civic affiliation is serving as a collegiate intern in the South Dakota State Legislature 20+ years ago. I am currently serving my fifth year on the Board of Directors for the Landing at Standley Lake HOA in Arvada. I have been an active volunteer at Meiklejohn Elementary, Oberon Middle and Ralston Valley High Schools.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

There are 527 homes in my neighborhood association. Social media has changed the way we relate to each other, for good and ill. Online tools have also enabled more communication and as President I have led the Board to solicit feedback from homeowners, be more transparent about decision-making and respond to members directly rather than through our management company when appropriate.

Statement

I bring a unique view to the commission in my non-partisan "averageness". I am not hyper political or connected in Colorado in any sense of the word. I represent a typical suburban voter by address but my business experience in the ag sector gives me an appreciation of rural interests as well. As a former economic analyst specializing in food systems and in antitrust issues, I know my way around a spreadsheet and database. I was the editor of the Texas Business Weekly in graduate school and take pride in efficient, clear communication. I offer a great combination of skills and experience and am willing to commit the time. Finally, my work on the commission is an investment for my kids in that my effort safe-guards the legitimacy of self-government in Colorado.

Statement on Working with Consensus

My experience at the most local level of government, my neighborhood HOA, is that people just want to be heard. They don't always agree with the outcome, but they appreciate being given fair consideration. With the Board itself, I have not sought unanimous decisions and I have lost some battles. But the Board can function through split decisions because we understand the position of each director, we all vote in good faith and we credit each Board member with good intentions. I value facts and reason over emotion in a debate, but a good sense of humor always helps.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My graduate coursework in Business utilized the Harvard case method where students continually applied analytical tools to ambiguous real-world situations. My professional work experience required distilling large datasets into specific financial and legal strategies. My volunteer activities have shown me how data, analysis and people all interconnect.
