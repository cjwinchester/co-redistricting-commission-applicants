Statement

My wife and I have lived in Colorado most of our lives. We have been retired for some time, are very healthy, hike and bike a lot, and enjoy many interests to fill our time. The love of our state and the health of it and our nation's sacred institutions is a great concern. I understand and abhor the damage and extreme inequity that the process of Gerrymandering has had upon our system of Democracy. I am honored to live in a state that has the foresight to develop a process for ending this inequity. This is but one of many other forces in play that are corrosive to our Democracy. I want to be a part of the strengthening of our state and our country's integrity through a stronger Democracy. This Commission is the means, and I humbly submit my experience and energy to that goal.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I am a person that believes that logical conclusion follows carefully presented fact in logical persons. I also believe that a fundamental fairness and understanding of the human condition is and should be embedded in logical conclusion. The combination of these things should guide me to impartial decision making and compromise with those who do not agree with my logical conclusion.
