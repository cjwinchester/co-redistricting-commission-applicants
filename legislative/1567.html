Statement

I have been a stay at home parent for the past six years. I have enjoyed nearly every minute of my time with my children, and I am not ready to return to the work force. But I do feel like my children have reached an age where some of my time and energy can be devoted to other things that I think are important. I don't think I have any particular skills or experience that make me a perfect candidate for this commission. I'm sure there are other people who are far more qualified than I am. But I am interested in contributing to my community and my state. I'm young and energetic. I think that sometimes what is missing in politics is the voice of an average citizen, with average knowledge and average concerns. I think I could be that person on the commission. I live in Douglas County, where my neighbors mainly vote straight republican on their ballots. I have voted for both parties on the same ballot, and some days that makes me feel like "the most liberal person in Highlands Ranch." I feel like the people in the middle sometimes have their voices drowned out by the louder voices on the edges. I would like to be a voice for the moderates of both parties on this commission.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe in all relationships, both personal and professional, it is vital to recognize when to stand your ground and when to pick your battles. The most important aspect of compromise is clear and effective communication to get to the heart of the issue for each individual on the commission. I will contribute to creating agreement among commissioners by continuing to discuss what's important to all parties until we are able to find a middle ground on issues.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have an accounting background and have worked with medium and small size businesses. I often think of most political issues from an economic perspective. Often questions are presented as "what is the cost of acting now?" But I think some people forget to ask "what is the cost of waiting?" Addressing the cause and effect of all possible choices, made or not made, is a skill that I would bring to the commission.
