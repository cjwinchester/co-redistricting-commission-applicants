Statement

I would like to serve on the commission because I ardently believe that voting is one of the most important rights that we exercise as citizens. As such, it is imperative that voting districts are an accurate reflection of their population. I also wish to serve in an attempt to make the commission more diverse: I am a woman and a minority, and I think I could bring a different perspective to the group as a result.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I will promote consensus, and contribute to creating agreement among commissioners, by listening to understand. I will strive to find common goals amongst commissioners, and building upon those shared values. In communication pledge to always be respectful, even if I might disagree. In my past job and life experiences I have demonstrated the ability to be fair and impartial. I am analytical, detail-oriented and wish to work toward a common goal.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

This required the application of logic and reason to varied problems. I also do the same in my work as a teacher: teachers regularly must use data and apply that knowledge to create individualized/personalized learning for students.
