Statement

I feel my training in urban and regional planning, with my emphasis on community development, provides a strong grounding in the redistricting process and rationale. My deep commitment and over 20 years of work on racial, social, economic, and gender equity will serve me and commission to ensure fair and equitable representation of all Coloradans in the redistricting process.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe in coming to this work from a foundation of common values, goals, and objectives - to make sure all Coloradans are fairly represented by their elected officials. I would hope we would start with establishing these common values, goals, and objectives as well as develop a guiding set of principles or criteria that would guide our decision-making. Should things get "hot" I would hope we would go back to these tools to take our emotions out of our decision-making. Fairness has always been a core value for me and in many ways, being guided by the desire for fairness leads me to be more impartial - to be fair means everyone is represented, regardless of my personal opinions.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Extensive experience analyzing data through my professional career - be it budgets or surveys. Indeed, it is from data that we can see patterns and themes and these can serve to guide our work towards fairness. Data is a great tool to support fairness and impartiality. It has its own logic and leads us to reason. You just need to look for the patterns.
