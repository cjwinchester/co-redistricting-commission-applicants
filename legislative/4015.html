Past Political Activity

Not politically active prior to 2016. From 2016 on, I have volunteered for 2 Lakewood City Council Members (Dana Guitwien and Shakti) and donated to both of them. Both within my state of Colorado and also a few (Democrats) outside my state.

Southern Gables Neighborhood Sustainability Committee, Neighborhood Watch Coordinator, Jefferson County Democrats, Indivisible Front Range Resistance, Indivisible CO-7, Colorado Move to Amend, Town Hall Project.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I have testified at the State Capitol on several occasions to support and/or against a variety of topics, to include: sustainability, gun control reforms, oil & gas setbacks, holding Colorado Government accountable, Affordable Housing, Full Day kindergarten, etc. Precinct Coordinator and Precinct Captain for Jeffco Dems. In this position, I have recruited fellow PCP's and others to assist with election related activities (and participated myself) in Phone Banking, Text Banking, Lit Dropping, Door Knocking, etc. I have also acted as the Chair for my Precinct Caucus for the past 2 Democratic Caucuses; as well as helping to plan the Caucus event itself.

Statement

I am committed to seeing Colorado's redistricting process be the most fair, and transparent, process possible. My hope is that Colorado will be used as a model of fairness for the nation. I believe that democracy (small d) can only survive if the process is fair and the people believe in its fairness and adhere to the results - even if they are unhappy with the results.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I do believe that everyone brings wisdom to an interaction and that the more points of view presented, discussed and valued, the better the ultimate solution. I have always been able to see both sides of an issue and am a strong advocate for all voices being heard; even the ones I may disagree with. I have a deep ability to empathize and understand others' points of view. I am always open to admitting that I could be wrong and willing to change my opinion, if the facts presented are logical.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My 30 year banking background provided me many opportunities to analyze, both numerically and socially, various problems and solutions to many issues. I understand that any issue contains both a "number" component and a "people" component; and I am always dedicated to resolving both. If a solution only addresses one of these, it will ultimately fail.
