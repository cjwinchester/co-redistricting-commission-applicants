Statement

I grew up on the Front Range and have spent most of my life since 1982 in Colorado's High Country (Chaffee, Summit, Eagle and Grand Counties). I feel my perspective as an independent voter and observer of the social and political changes across the state can be of use in bringing a contemplative stance to discussions on redistricting to best serve all the residents of Colorado.

With a background including mediation training and my regular contact with folks coming from a wide spectrum of viewpoints, I feel that I can help people find common ground and find ways to agree.

I like to step back and look at a bigger picture when encountering a problem. Trying not to answer something immediately (even if I think I know what should be done). This has been helpful in getting buy-in from others once I do share my perspective.
