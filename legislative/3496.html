Statement

I want to preserve representative democracy from becoming a casualty of partisan politics. I want to serve my state and country in an official capacity. I believed strongly in the 2018 ballot initiative that created this commission, and enthusiastically voted for it.

I will promise to listen to all relevant viewpoints and analyze them fairly and impartially. I will not bring a pre-conceived agenda to the commission. I will evaluate all relevant data and evidence with an open mind and will promise to engage in respectful dialogue with others on the commission about how to apply that information to our decisions.
