Statement

My reason for applying to the commission is more of a calling than a desire per se. At the risk of coming off conceited, I want someone like me on the commission. I want our commissioners to be fair and impartial. I want our commissioners to care about representing all Coloradans and not be focused on partisan gains. Given that I am capable and possess the qualities necessary to aid in the success of the commission, I feel called to apply to serve. While I would be honored to serve on the commission, I hope that the commission is representative. It is important that we have a multi-cultural commission leading the redistricting efforts. I care more about this than I do about being selected myself.

Statement on Working with Consensus

When those in power seek to tip the scales in their favor, it undermines the democratic values we are supposed to uphold in this nation. It does not establish justice; insure domestic tranquility; promote the general warfare; nor does it secure the blessings of liberty to ourselves and our posterity. Gerrymandering is a perversion of justice and is antithetical to the principles set by our forefathers in the creation of the United States. I have been called for jury duty three times and have been selected for juries three times. Perhaps what comes though during the jury selection process is that I will be as impartial as possible. Being honest, fair, and just is inherent to my nature. What I will bring to the table is a desire to be fair and ensure those who historically have been underrepresented are protected in the redistricting. My career success to date is largely due to the strengths of relationships I have built with internal colleagues and external partners. I believe I have been able to effectively lead cross-functional teams due to my ability to earn the trust of others which is largely owed to my dependability, sincerity, and friendly nature. I do not have any concerns about my ability to quickly build relationships with my fellow commissioners nor do I have any doubt that I can prompt consensus because I have plenty of examples from both my career and personal life. I am pragmatic, persuasive, and capable of working with people who bring a variety of opinions and goals.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Every position I have held required a strong aptitude for analysis. I have examined TV sales to determine promotional pricing, studied consumers’ consumption habits to derive insights about brand loyalty, forecasted sales, and evaluated financial performance against P&L goals. To be an effective product manager I had to identify, understand, and anticipate the needs of my customers. I have a proven track record of using analytical skills to solve problems across countless unique circumstances. Being able to analyze data that comes out of the census results and applying that to the mapping out districts sounds fascinating to me.
