Professional Background

I have a lot of experience in advocating for people and some experience in organizing and representing organization. In my time with the District Attorney's Office, I have had considerable experience advocating and speaking with people from all walks of life. Statement

I would like to play a part in the redrawing of the congressional and legislative districts. I think it is my civic duty to contribute where I can, and I can here.

Statement on Working with Consensus

My job as a district attorney is to create agreement between people by presenting them with facts. My job also requires a fairness and impartiality for the effective and efficient administration of the justice system. My time on the board of the Pro Bono Project also helped me develop skills in promoting consensus among professional organizations that have diverse and sometimes competing interests in order to attain a common goal. With the Grand Valley Ducks Unlimited chapter I contributed by soliciting donations from various organizations and businesses and also took recommendations from various stakeholders in the community to the board for consideration.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As an attorney I have spent years developing my analytical skills. Applying logic and reason to problems is also a daily task for me. While making a decision for bringing criminal charges, I have look at the facts presented to me and analyze how they fit within the laws of the state. I often have to make determinations based upon logical conclusions that can be drawn from the information presented to me prior to that decision. I also bring the logic and reason to solving any problems I encounter as a parent. Recently there was a safety concern at my child's daycare and my wife and I brought to the attention of the supervisory staff and requested a meeting. In an area where it is easy to get emotional and defensive, there were no raised voices or short tempers; just conversation and understanding that lead to a resolution all parties could support.
