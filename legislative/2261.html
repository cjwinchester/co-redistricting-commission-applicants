Background

Professional Background

As an undergraduate studying political science, I extensively studied descriptive representation as it relates to all communities and constituencies having a seat at the table. My Master's capstone project was with the City of Littleton, CO assessing and providing recommendations based on a community survey distributed to businesses and residents. I have managed volunteers from various constituencies, interest groups, and organizations during my time as a Campaign Manager.

Past Political Activity

I have been a precinct committee person. In 2012, I was Deputy Campaign Manager and Legislative Intern for Evie Hudak for State Senate. In 2013, I was Campaign Manager for Monica Duran for Wheat Ridge City Council, and Legislative Intern for State Senator Evie Hudak. In 2014, I was Campaign Manager and Legislative Aide for Rachel Zenzinger for State Senate. I have donated to the following candidates since 2015: Hillary Clinton for President, Rachel Zenzinger for State Senate, Brianna Titone for State Representative, Kamala Harris for President, Julian Castro for President, Elizabeth Warren for President, Joe Biden for President, Amy Klobuchar for President, Andy Kerr for County Commissioner, Tracy Kraft-Tharp for County Commissioner, John Hickenlooper for US Senate, Raphael Warnock for US Senate, and Jon Ossoff for US Senate.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Precinct Committee Person in the Jefferson County Democratic Party.

Statement

In my free time, I enjoy using Dave's Redistricting App to create districts. I enjoy both maps and geography and political data, and I believe I have the skills and knowledge of Colorado's political geography to be a valuable member of the commission. Most importantly, I believe strongly in community representation, and I I believe that redistricting is one of the most pivotal acts in our democracy. As a multi-ethnic gay man and Millennial, with both white and Latino heritage, I believe I can provide an important voice on the commission.

Statement on Working with Consensus

While I am a Democrat, I believe in being fair when it comes to redistricting, and making sure that representation is proportional to the voting behavior of our state, while allowing for some competitive districts as well. I strongly value compromise, negotiation, and believe in having everyone's voice heard.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I understand what criteria goes into the redistricting process, such as near equal population, contiguousness, keeping counties and municipalities as intact and together as possible, proportionality, minority representation, and compactness.
