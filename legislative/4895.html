Statement on Working with Consensus

I am a firm believer in consensus-based decision making in both my professional and personal life. As a senior executive in various roles, I have been and am committed to a democratic and civic-minded approach to decisions, based on active listening, empathy, and consideration of different perspectives. I have strong opinions but I believe I am fair and impartial. I am a staunch Democrat but I am an ever stronger advocate of everyone's right to be heard, everyone's right to vote, and everyone's right to be fairly represented.
