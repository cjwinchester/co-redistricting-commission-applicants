Statement

I think its important to build a fair and representative Colorado, and I am proud that Colorado continues to take so many steps forward as a state in creating equal opportunities for everyone. I am proud to live in Colorado and I want to be involved in making this state better, I think it is important to work within your community and server where you can. I am not generally a political person but I think it is important to connect and advocate for everyone. Colorado is a great example of democracy and I want to see it continue.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I strive to be a good team member in most aspects. I think varying viewpoints are important and valuable in life. I tend to be a rational and pragmatic person who works well with others. I have a long history of collaboration and building communities and I think this will translate well to working on the commission.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I tend to be an analytical person, I like to inspect issues and problems from all angles. I think you can always learn more and do better. My approach to most things is to assume that there is another angle that I need to learn. I like to listen to all arguments, read everything I can and make as strongly informed decision as possible. I do my bust to use every tool I have available to find the best approach.
