Background

Past Political Activity

I have been involved in volunteer activities, including organizing for Julian Castro and Elizabeth Warren in Colorado in this most recent cycle. I also organized for Mark Udall in 2014, Hillary Clinton and down ballot Democrats in 2016, and for local races in 2018. I have donated to Hillary Clinton, Julian Castro, and Elizabeth Warren.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I was on the volunteer fundraising board of Denver Food Rescue (known as the Leadership Circle) for approximately four years until this past September, when I stepped down because I started working for them. I have also been a volunteer with the Denver Museum of Nature and Science, Emily Griffith Technical College teaching ESL to refugees, and the Morrison Natural History Museum.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I was born and raised in Denver and have also lived in Durango, Rifle, and Glenwood Springs. I have worked for the Bureau of Land Management on the western slope as well as a variety of roles in the Denver area, culminating in my position with Denver Food Rescue. I have interacted with and built relationships in many communities across the state, from western land owners to refugee communities along East Colfax.

Statement

I am always looking for new ways to serve my home state, which I care about deeply. I believe I can bring a wide range of local knowledge as well as a millennial's viewpoint to this commission.

Statement on Working with Consensus

In my current role, I work with communities throughout the Denver Metro area to source and distribute food through no cost grocery programs. Each of our communities has a unique set of challenges, and my role is to ensure that we are all able to work together towards a common goal of providing fresh, healthy food to communities experiencing food apartheid in Denver. Prior to that, I had long experience working on collaborative scientific problems, including publishing reports with dozens of contributors. I also served in student government, including as president of my college, in graduate school.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I hold a masters degree and a PhD in a field which required me to analyze maps, statistics, and a wide range of other factual information to make my arguments. In my current role, I use these skills nearly every day to solve fast-arising problems relating to food distribution in the Denver area.
