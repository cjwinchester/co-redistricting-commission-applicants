Statement

A representative democracy only works if everyone is represented fairly. Gerrymandering has skewed our districts so that true representation is impossible in many districts around the country. I am proud of Colorado for being proactive and a leader among the United States for subjects as wide-ranging as election security and marijuana legalization. I am eager to participate in the process as we work to create independent redistricting standards that fairly represent Colorado and can serve as an example for the rest of the country.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe that every idea is worth considering, and I work diligently in my professional and personal lives to foster communication between different views. Everyone's perspective sheds light on distinct aspects of the truth and can illuminate strengths and weaknesses in any given plan. Then I create criteria lists based on those ideas and develop alternatives to conflict points. The most important thing about remaining fair in any conflict is recognizing that true impartiality is illusory. The best way to find fairness is with scrupulous attention to my own biases and my team's input.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am a critical and pragmatic thinker and base my decisions on meaningful data. If I don't have pertinent or current information provided, I can research and parse relevant information and adapt it to the task at hand.
