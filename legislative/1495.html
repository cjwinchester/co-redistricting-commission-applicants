Statement

This election has been a reminder of the importance of each and every vote and the right of every vote to count. The importance of drawing new districts in a way that is fair for every voter in Colorado is equally important. Although I advocate for my clients, I do so in a way that is objective and fair. I would like to apply my analytical skills in an impartial way that appreciates Colorado's diversity.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I help negotiate agreements with clients who have intractable positions that are odds with other parties whether in a transaction or the settlement of litigation. But, I have been successful (in part) because am able to work hard to find each party's true interests and find a resolution. My integrity, professionalism, objectivity, and reputation for being fair and honest is how I make my living and I guard that reputation zealously. I serve on an international trade association's ethics committee in large part because I have shown that I am impartial in investigating conflict of interest claims and allegations of violations of professionalism. I have also recently been appointed to serve on the Jefferson County Cultural Commission for the same reasons.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I routinely investigate disputes to develop a full factual record, research the issues fully so I can advise my clients of its options, advocate for my clients in a transaction or in court. I routinely work with detailed statistical reports, documents, expert reports and investigations, and make determinations as to determining the weight that any such documents or witnesses should be given. I believe that these analytical skills will be useful in working to develop the redistricting.
