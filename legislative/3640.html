Statement

I have been involved with redistricting efforts for three cycles, 2011, 2001, and 1991 because of my leadership roles in Democratic politics on the Western Slope. Although this was under the "old" system of redistricting, I welcome working on this new method of redrawing district lines for both the legislature and congress. My census background will also help because I have a deeper understanding of the social divisions, as well as political, here on the Western Slope.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have been successful in occupations that involve sales, fundraising, management, and persuasion. If I had not been willing to listen, compromise, and build consensus, I would not have been in leadership roles in social organizations, as well as sought out for input on redistricting efforts in the past.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

During my recent campaign for county commissioner, I had to learn the issues, form opinions based in fact, participate in community forums where I had to defend my opinions, understand expenditures in a $117 million county budget, and persuade people to vote for me. This experience will be valuable as a committee member for redistricting.
