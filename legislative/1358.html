Professional Background

I focus on concrete, hands-on, data-driven efforts to help further causes that I believe in. For example, for several years I have led a team that has collected breeding bird data at a Critical Wildlife Habitat in Boulder County. I analyze and present this data to Town and County staff, boards, and commissions to help inform decisions about management and development around this area. Recently, this effort significantly contributed to a new water management strategy that meets the needs of both wildlife and downstream irrigation users.

Statement

I am a fervent believer in voting fairness. In recent years, it seems that partisan interests in many parts of the country have developed a win-at-all-costs mentality rather than focusing on doing the right thing for the voting public. I know that the CO redistricting commission will have a practical, significant, and long-lasting impact on ensuring that each Coloradan’s vote counts in a meaningful way, and I deeply wish to have a role in that process. I am convinced that serving on this commission is the most consequential way I can make a difference for fair voting in our state. Construction of districts to match the statewide electoral balance supports the idea of “one person - one vote,” and creating competitive districts makes our elected representatives more responsive to the wishes of the entire electorate and discourages hyperpartisanship.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Even though I am a “Front Range liberal,” I grew up in a deeply conservative area and my family has been supported by agriculture and timber for several generations. This background helps give me an empathetic perspective on the economic and political perspectives of people from other parts of the state. In another context, an important part of my last job was to design a supercomputer that had a fixed budget and had to meet the needs of a wide variety of researchers. I interviewed dozens of faculty from numerous departments across several universities and contributed to a final design that took all of their requirements into account. I then led the development of a scheduling system that ensured fair access for all of these users. Not everyone got everything they wanted, but they all appreciated that their interests were taken into account so thoroughly.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

After several years as a practicing theoretical physicist, and a subsequent career as a cyberinfrastructure expert, I feel that numeracy, logical thinking, and data-based analysis are a part of my very being! I am comfortable using a number of software packages for data analysis and modeling, and have even created several of these programs myself.
