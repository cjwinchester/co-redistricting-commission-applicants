Statement

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a CPA I have learned to look at issues objectively and explain situations to clients in a balanced and reasonable manner helping them to understand their options and make decisions that will be best under the circumstances. I think this ability is useful in promoting consensus or agreement. As for the ability to be fair and impartial, this too, I believe is a characteristic of a CPA. I have always and continue to strive for such throughout life.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Analytical skills, logic, and reason are all skills I believe I possess being a CPA. Having worked in both taxation and auditing I have dealt with many clients and businesses over the years, including the Internal Revenue Service. One must be able to switch gears very rapidly and effectively when dealing with a high volume of clients at any one point in time, all having their own specific issues, while at the same time looking forward as to how decisions made today will affect the future.
