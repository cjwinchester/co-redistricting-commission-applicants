Statement

I would like to represent my fellow Coloradans who are like me because my age group lacks representation on all levels of government. I grew up in Colorado and would like to serve as a voice for young Coloradans.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Working in community organizing for a non-partisan non-profit has challenged me to see everything from both sides. I have heard opinions from every side of certain issues. While I have my own opinions about issues in our state, I don't feel it would be fair to push those onto the commission or in this position at all. I find myself to be a level-headed person and as a student at MSU Denver, I study rhetoric. I understand techniques of persuasion and that emotions tend to trump logic with political issues and in general. I do not believe it is partisan to think that every Coloradan should be treated equally as a person and as a voter and the importance of representation of all people in government.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I consider myself to think of societal issues in terms of equity, which I believe comes from logic. A healthy, functioning society recognizes inequality and addresses it.
