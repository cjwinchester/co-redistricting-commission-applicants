Background

Past Political Activity

I have donated to the Bernie Sanders campaign and I have heavily supported (with rhetoric and argument, not money) the political action group RepresentUS which seeks to implement ranked choice voting, and end gerrymandering. I firmly believe that the biggest problem our country faces is the partisanship inherent in a two party system, and the drawing of voter districts by those parties is a big contributor to that problem.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

RepresentUS is the only political organization I have belonged to. RepresentUS is nonpartisan and focused on promoting exactly the kind of change our voters demonstrated is important to them here.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I don't have any experience organizing groups, organizations, or associations. However, as an attorney, representation and advocacy are essential parts of my chosen profession.

Statement

I strongly believe that partisanship and corruption are the biggest problems our nation faces. The redistricting commission is a huge win for Colorado in the fight against that partisanship and corruption, and I want to be a part of implementing those solutions and addressing those problems.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe that promoting consensus in this situation needs to be based on sound logic and rationale. I believe that the majority of the country agrees on the principles of fairness, as long as they aren't in a partisan mindset. The key to remaining impartial is to remember that impartiality is the goal. In my profession, one of the most important skills I can have is the ability to put myself in the shoes of the opposing side and try to truly understand their motivation, logic, and feelings. When you can understand and even agree with the logic and motivation that they are using, it's generally easy to persuade them on the basis of the common ground that exists between their view and my own. One of the roles I really enjoy in my area of law is working as a mediator to resolve differences between opposing views instead of advocating for one side or the other. Even in advocating for my own clients, I find that pointing out the strengths of the opposing view is one of the most important parts of persuading my client to find common ground and compromise.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I hold undergraduate degrees in Molecular, Cellular, Developmental Biology and Philosophy. Both of those degrees involve high levels of analysis, logic, and reason. My favorite classes in Philosophy were Symbolic Logic and Philosophy of Language. My enjoyment of those classes is what led me to pursue my law degree. I apply these analytical and reasoning skills in persuading the Court, and more often my client to see things the logical rational way in which I see them.
