Background

Professional Background

Over the years I have been a member of the local areaBLM advisory council, a member of the Pueblo Community College Board, active in the Friends of the Library, local staffer for Senator Bill Armstrong, hosted a local radio talk show for a few months, was GOP county chairman back in the 70's - did assorted newsletters and worked on various campaigns as a volunteer.

Past Political Activity

Donated to the Republican Party on the local, state and national levels. Contributed to a few campaigns although am not in a position to send a lot of money to any candidates.

Republican Precinct Committeewoman, Rosemount Museum Auxiliary and volunteer tour guide

Statement

I understand the local area well, having lived here since 1971. I am well acquainted with the surrounding area including Fremont, Custer,the Arkansas valley communities and others. I realize that a legislative district must have a representative tha is familiar with the whole district and the boundaries of the district will either make this possible, or impossible, depending on what is included.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I can be impartial politically, and generally speaking can use my sense of humor to soften the edges of the debate, but I will be firm in my belief that the district should represent a cross section of the population and it's interests .

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My analytical skills will probably equal or surpass those of the folks who read this application. I remember the process from the last redistricting that resulted in a district that runs from the San Luis Valley, takes in some territory in Pueblo and goes down to the Arkansas valley. I would hope the next group could come up with a better solution.
