Statement

Gerrymandering for political outcomes deprives the people of their voice. I believe the interests of democracy are best served when we have competitive districts that represent geographically and culturally coherent regions. I have hope that these commissions can ensure that the districts are fair and representative. Voters chose their representatives, not the other way around.

Statement on Working with Consensus

You gain trust through transparency, honesty, and communication. A good rule for large meetings is that no one speaks twice until everyone speaks once. Everyone has bias, of course. I tend to favor Democrats. But my interest in this commission is to draw coherent, representative districts that aren't engineered for a particular outcome. I think we all realize that redistricting has become a political game in some states. We can set the example to follow. This isn't that hard, as long as our intentions are pure.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have a background in science and geography, software engineering, and GIS. I rely on my analytical skills daily to solve technical problems in software that designs and manages facility data for utilities. This would be a different application of the same skill set.
