Statement

I believe that any system, particularly one tasked with creating a fair, level political playing field for voters, is only as good as the integrity of those who design it, and only effective to the degree that it aligns with high ethical principles. As someone who often finds myself in the "common sense middle" on most issues, I believe I can add a valuable perspective and skill set to the commission.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a non-profit leader, management trainer and support group facilitator for over 25 years, I have learned the value of listening to all voices in the room, considering fresh perspectives, and treating individuals with respect, even when we disagree. Though I am not personally into astrology, several friends have said that my ability to see both sides of an issue stems from the fact that I am a Gemini. Whatever the case, I am a mission-focused individual who is known for parsing the personal from the business at hand, and guiding/facilitating boards on which I have served through some difficult discussions. That said, once I have formed a logical, well-supported opinion on an issue, I am an articulate advocate, which is also useful in building consensus and agreement.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My background includes training in philosophy, statistics for social sciences, and eight years handling medical-legal claims in the private and public sectors, which involved gathering information, applying relevant statutes to existing facts, and articulating and prioritizing issues in order of priority or logical order in order to achieve a goal. My work in the wireless industry, along with maintaining websites and social media accounts for non-profits, requires constant learning and rethinking systems and processes in order to stay current.
