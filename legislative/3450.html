Past Political Activity

In 2016 I knocked on doors for Hilary Clinton's presidential campaign. In 2018 I knocked doors for the state senate campaigns of Tammy Story and Faith Winter. I worked on the campaigns of Matt Jones and Mike Foote for both state representative and state senator, and for Jones county commissioner campaign. I've been a Democratic precinct leader for most of the past 30 years. In 2020 I worked on the Blue Wave Postcard campaign. I make a monthly donation to the Boulder County Democratic Party and since 2015 have donated to Our Revolution, Doug Jones, Paula Dickerson, Dr. Al Gross, John Hickenlooper, Mark Kelly, Diane Mitch Bush, Let America Vote, Steve Bullock, John Tester, Indivisible Action, Ike McCorkle, Chris Kolker, Sally Boccella, Joe Biden, LCV Victory Fund, Callie Rennison, Fair Tax Colorado, Jonathan Singer, Andrew Romanoff, Elizabeth Warren, Phil Weiser, Cory Booker, Alice Madden, Jared Polis, Michael Bennett, House Majority Project, Matt Jones, Mike Foote, DSCC, Andrew Gillum, Bernie Sanders, Dave Young, Jason Crow, Tammy Story, Karen McCormick, Matt Gray, Stacey Abrams, Move On, Joe Neguse, Sonya Jaquez Lewis, and Tracy Burnett.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

Political advocacy was summarized above. My career at the University of Colorado was dedicated to the access and retention of low income, first generation college students and students from underrepresented backgrounds. My early career and my late-career professional work focused on improving education for Native American students and the development of tribal colleges. For many years I've volunteered on projects for Volunteers for Outdoor Colorado and served on boards related to resource conservation and recycling. I also served on the board of the Boulder County I Have A Dream Foundation, helping to smooth the educational pathway of low income young people.

Statement

I strongly support the concept of an independent, transparent commission to set fair, non-gerrymandered boundaries state political districts.

Statement on Working with Consensus

During a long career I negotiated for budgets and infrastructure, settled disputes among staff members, developed consensus for departmental reorganizations, made difficult decisions about admitting students the our flagship university, and chaired an alliance advocating for increasing diversity at the university. My communication abilities and reputation for civility and fairness stood me in good stead. I do not possess a winner-take-all mentality. I strive to advocate for my principles while respecting the values and needs of others. I don't think I have all the answers.
