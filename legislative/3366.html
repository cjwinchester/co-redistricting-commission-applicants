Statement

A commission such as this should be founded on good listening. In my 30-year engineering career, I was required to appear frequently before public agency boards and the public while I was resident in Nevada, Michigan, Florida, and Colorado to explain complex engineering problems and solutions in understandable terms. I also spent 2-1/2 years in London, UK working in the engineering and construction sectors and was responsible for change management across a wide variety of technical and non-technical challenges. Through all this experience, I have found that good listening skills are absolutely necessary to have the highest potential for developing shared values, goals, and solutions in groups, committees, and commissions. My wife and I have raised four kids and can attest to the importance of being good listeners, especially during the teenage years. I believe all of this experience provides me with the necessary listening and coalition building skills to add value to this commission.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I will do my utmost to work towards consensus and agreement among the members of the commission. I am a firm believer that progress only comes through fair compromise and I have a proven skill set in that area that I would like to leverage for the benefit of Colorado and its residents.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am trained as an engineer, I base decisions on logic and data as much as possible; my experience tells me that the non-technical and emotional concerns must also be considered to bring holistic and sustainable solutions. I will bring all of these problem solving skills to this public service opportunity and would be honored to serve.
