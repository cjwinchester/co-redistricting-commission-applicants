Statement

I would like to serve on this commission because I devote a very significant amount of time to the political process, and I would like to continue to do so for the long term. Efforts to redistrict are a large concern of mine and will likely have a significant impact on the district where I live - District 3.

Statement on Working with Consensus

The highest priority for the organization I started in 2016 (and continue to run) besides voter engagement was to try to "bridge the divide." I believe our greatest work at this time is preserving our democracy, and the only way this can be done is if we work together and listen to one another, taking everyone's needs into account and without the division of partisanship. I believe there is a significant opportunity for bipartisan action and collaboration by citizens within the state of Colorado. We have a lot of work to do, and we need to start yesterday. Please see our policy statements on the D3 Colorado website. I was the co-author of these, and Senator Bennet said that these policy statements were some of the best he's ever seen - https://d3colorado.org/issues#together-we-stand

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am non confrontational, a good listener and I listen to logic, reason and facts before making decisions. I do not operate from an emotional standpoint, but rather let information and the truth be my driving force. I don't jump to conclusions. I take my time before I make up my mind until I feel like I have all of the information.
