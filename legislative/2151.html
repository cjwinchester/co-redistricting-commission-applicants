Statement

I have always believed that redistricting should represent the needs of a community and not the needs of a party or the desires of an incumbent. Looking at ways districts are often drawn to maintain power in the antithesis of the democratic/representative process that we need in Colorado and throughout the US. In order to make sure actual communities are represented, we need fair districting. This can only be achieved with bipartisan commissions.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I am used to working in sensitive situations. As an ICU nurse, I am trusted to ease people through the most critical periods of their lives, whether the outcome is restored health or the end of life. I am used to putting the patient and family first, and I believe this will translate. As a nurse educator, I also give calm and formative feedback to students in high pressure situations. I understand the need to be both diplomatic and to give helpful feedback.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have been a nurse since 1989 and in ICU since 1993. I have also taught student nurses in the hospital environment since 2010. My experience has given me the ability to closely monitor situations and make changes to maintain and improve a complicated and ever changing situation. My CNL degree and certification involves applying quality and safety issues within a highly complex environment.
