Statement

I want to serve on the commission because I think I would do a good job, and benefit Coloradans. As an Urban Planner, I have long been passionate about how people organize themselves into communities and districts, and how invisible human boundaries can either support or undermine that sense of community. I also believe that democracy functions best on a level playing field, and I want to offer my skills to ensure that it happens.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a developer, I frequently have to negotiate contracts between parties whose interests may not align. I focus on needs-driven creation of solutions that work for all parties, as opposed to solution-driven arguments that favor one side. I prioritize functioning government that represents the will of the people over the ideologies of a specific party, and as such, I would listen carefully to make sure that the needs of all interested parties are being addressed.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My work centers around financial analysis of scenarios - generating and analysing solutions to find options that work best for the project at hand. I am also familiar with working with census data. As an undergrad, I trained in the sciences, and developed critical thinking skills that I use to this day.
