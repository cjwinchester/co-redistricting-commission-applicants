Background

Professional Background

My experience, training and dedication to transparency, fairness and equal access to representation by elected bodies has been my personal commitment. I will provide critical thinking and outstanding problem solving skills with a collaborative leadership style to a team effort to best meet the needs of Coloradoans. I have worked as an advocate for the underserved and people of color throughout my personal and professional career. As a community organizer and advocate I have served on Denver Health and Hospital Board, Latin American Research and Service Agency (LARSA); one of organizers of the Hispanic Agenda in 1987.

Past Political Activity

Elected and served as a Colorado State Legislator from 1985-1992; Appointed as Regional Administrator for Region 8 Housing and Urban Development first term Clinton Administration Appointed as Regional Administrator for Rural Housing Services Rural Development USDA for Obama Administration 2nd term

Statement

I bring a good good set of policy analysis, strategic planning, problem solving and intercultural communication skills that facilitate my ability to work with people of different backgrounds, and political perspectives. Serving in the minority party of the General Assembly while elected as a State Representative I was known as a moderate with ability to work both sides of the aisle and was quite successful in passing major legislation to improve the lives of people in Colorado. I am fair and work well to achieve a consensus that benefits the state while successfully advocating for people of color, women and the underserved. I bring a great set of facilitation, analysis, problem solving and consensus building skills to help the commission achieve its mission . Earlier in my career I served as a Senior Analysist with the Colorado Legislative Council a nonpartisan organization which added to my ability to be nonpartisan and fair. I believe I will add significant value because of my dedication to fairness, transparency and advocacy for people of color, women and underserved populations using my critical thinking and problem solving skills.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Using my ability to motivate, and create teams with a shared inspired vision to be fair and transparent I will facilitate the efforts of the commission to meet its mission with positive value of each person getting equal and equitable ability to have representation.
