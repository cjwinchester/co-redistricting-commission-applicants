Statement

My community is my heart. I truly believe that voting is the strong form of power anyone has. Everyone should have be able to vote and have access to voting. I want to be on the commission because I think it helps find a way to make sure everyone has a voice.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I will listen to people. Most often when people don't feel understood or feel ignored they become angry. When let someone know you hear them even if you don't agree; they will be more willing to hear and understand your point of view.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I remember when I ran city council a few years ago. I knocked on the door of a hard core republican. He told me I was to liberal and he would never vote for me. I asked he to please share with me his vision for our community. After listing to him I repeated back what he had told me. I wanted to know I had heard what he said. I asked if he would listen to me and my point of view. He did; I then told him that we worked together we could accomplish a lot more. Not only did he vote for me but he learned to view some issues a little differently. I truly believe that when listen we are able to be more understanding and caring towards one another. I will bring open ears and an open mind to the table.
