Statement

I believe representation of the people of Colorado and the nation should be based on votes from the people. I do not believe that representation should be altered due to politicians and special interests being able to gerrymander districts in ways that are advantageous to a particular party.

Statement on Working with Consensus

While I do have opinions about politics and policies in general, I have never been registered to vote with either of the two major parties because I have never felt either fully represents me. I understand that there are many different opinions about many different issues, and those opinions do not have to be mutually exclusive. Where those opinions are mutually exclusive, I feel I would be fair. My experience as a hearing officer for unemployment hearings helped me to hone my ability to be fair, within the law, and impartial to all parties involved.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

During my studies at DU law, I regularly had to read and analyze case law, statutes, and other rules of law. As a hearing officer, I further utilized these skills by gathering facts from various parties and applying logic, reason and law in order to write sound legal decisions.
