Statement on Working with Consensus

I am a proud native of Pueblo but have had the unique opportunity to spend time in many different communities throughout Colorado. Currently with my position with the USOPC, and through my work with the institute of internal auditors I have had the opportunity to interact with people residing in Colorado Springs. As an auditor for Colorado East Bank & Trust (now TBK Bank) , I had the privilege of getting to know many people who live and work in rural communities, bedroom communities, and in urban areas. It gave me an understanding and appreciation for how the needs of Granada, Colorado might be different than the needs of Pueblo or even Severance. My work as internal auditor has given me the ability to review information in an objective and unbiased manner. Ensuring integrity and transparency so the conclusions go unchallenged.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As an auditor I am often called upon to analyze and interpret various types of data, in rare instances the data is very black and white and very little judgement is needed. More often, the data being reviewed is subject to interpretation and requires a high level of knowledge and understanding in order to draw conclusions. It is also important that the principals of logic are applied in order to assert and defend any conclusions drawn after the data has been analyzed.
