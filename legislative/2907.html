Statement

I think this is an opportunity to engage with a critically important local and statewide issue. I have had conducted international research focused on community engagement and empowerment. I am keen to serve in my own state to use my geospatial skills to develop the next iteration of redistricting maps.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I think being flexible and adaptable is an essential role for this position as well as a willingness to listen and engage with individuals with differing opinions. I have had to work in numerous multi-cultural environments where issues related to resource use have had to be negotiated. Activities that include active discussion, sharing ideas, and engaging in negotiation are all key to creating agreements. Building relationships that are based on mutual respect and trust are essential to this process.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have been a university professor for 25 years. I have had to work with numerous individuals across all age groups and from different backgrounds. I am a geographer who uses geospatial tools and analysis to provide input for data-driven decision making. Maps are a common language and provide the basis for interactive discussion, presenting scenarios. and finding common ground.
