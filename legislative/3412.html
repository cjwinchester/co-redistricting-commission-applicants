Background

Past Political Activity

I have assisted in door to door solicitations, phone banks and lit distribution for the last two campaigns to elect Jason Crow, and the recent campaign to elect David Ortiz in State House District 38. I have assisted Democratic campaigns by distributing literature in all campaigns. I have donated money to various campaigns (Jason Crow, David Ortiz, Chris Kolker, and others I don't recall), all in the $25 - $50 range.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Nob Hill/Ridgeview Hills Civic Association - I help deliver quarterly newsletters. Director of Denver Cribbage Club #34.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I was Cubmaster of my son's local Cub Scout pack and served on the committee of his Boy Scout Troop. I coached my children's soccer teams in the Littleton Soccer Association for 13 years. I coached my son's baseball team for 3 years. I worked in public education for 35 years, as a regular ed and special ed teacher and as a school psychologist. I taught at Regis University for 3 years. During my teaching years, I was very active in teacher association leadership, organizing and advocating. I served as a faculty representative, member of the board of directors and, for one year, president of the Littleton Education Association. Until the pandemic, I was director of my local cribbage club, which required organizing, representing and advocating on a very small scale (club membership was 30 - 40 people).

Statement

I am interested in serving the community. The community needs to insure that votes are equal, according to the dictum one person, one vote. I can help with that. The process of establishing/redrawing districts sounds interesting.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have always been able to listen and discuss difficult topics with persons with whom I disagree. When I was president of the teacher association, I was able to work with the school board and the association members to reach accord on various topics. As director of the cribbage club, I had to resolve various conflictual issues - members always trusted me to be fair and impartial.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am an Eagle Scout. I earned a BS, two MA degrees and a PhD. For the PhD I wrote a dissertation that required an hypothesis, single-subject experimental design to verify the hypothesis, and statistical analysis to determine verification. I believe that logic and problem-solving are major strengths of mine.
