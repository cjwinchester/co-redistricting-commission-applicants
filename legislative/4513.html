Statement on Working with Consensus

I pride myself in my ability to make a connection with all types of individuals, from very diverse backgrounds, and occupying disparate positions with disparate levels of power, and identify what is important to them and how to get the best out of them. I am the third born out of four and I have always been in tune with making sure that processes and results are fair. When I lead a discussion or project, I am sensitive to the fact that everyone should have the opportunity to be heard, participate, and provide input and feedback, and I do my best to make sure that occurs. If I am not heading up the effort and am a participant, I will speak up to make sure all are heard and stand up to anyone who is dominating in an unfair or counterproductive manner.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have been practicing law for 20 years in the areas of criminal litigation, civil litigation, contract negotiation and drafting, business formation along with creating governing documents and partner agreements. I have been a strong advocate as well as an unbiased facilitator. I have assisted clients in forging governing documents for complex business entities, untangling contentious business disputes, and defending professional malpractice claims in Colorado courts. As a respite from the practice of law, I coach lacrosse. One of these positions was as head coach at Arapahoe High School from 2009-2015 where the program average 120 athletes, 5 teams, and a staff of 15 coaches. During my time as head of the program, I led them to their first and only 3 Colorado State Championships in 2009, 2012, and 2013. I worked with the coaches and athletes to identify individual and team strengths and weaknesses, and successfully devised training and regimens to capitalize on the strengths and minimize the weaknesses.
