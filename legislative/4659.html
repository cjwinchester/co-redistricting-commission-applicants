Statement on Working with Consensus

I do not vote straight down party lines. I vote according to issues that I think are best for my current living situation. I don't think how I vote for an issue or person would be appropriate for someone living in a different part of the state, especially as their economic, agricultural, and environmental situation could be drastically different and could call for a completely different solution. In other words, what works for residents in El Paso County, may not work for residents in Boulder County. I love to motivate people into looking at all sides of a problem and I'm good at doing so I've been told.
