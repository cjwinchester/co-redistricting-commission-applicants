Background

Past Political Activity

I’ve donated to the Bernie Sanders campaign in the 2016 and 2020 primaries, as well as the Hillary Clinton and Joe Biden campaigns in the 2016 and 2020 general elections. I have also knocked on doors for the Bernie Sanders campaign in 2020.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I am an elected board member of the Colorado Rail Passenger Association (since 2017) and an elected council member of the national Rail Passengers’ Association (since 2016). I am also a member of the Sierra Club and the American Association of Geographers.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

My primary experience in representing and advocating for the interests of Coloradans is through my involvement with rail advocacy. On my own time and personal expense, I have met with members of the Colorado’s delegations in the Senate and House in Washington to highlight the importance of both existing passenger rail in rural Colorado, as well as much-needed new service along the Front Range to create transportation options for Coloradans who do not have access to a personal vehicle. These experiences have taught me the importance of finding and using a political voice to advocate for important causes.

Statement

Serving on the Commission would allow me to combine my knowledge of state geography, experience in data analytics, and my political interest to help ensure that maps are drawn in a way thar reflects both the political and geographic diversity of our state. I believe that fairly drawn political maps are the foundation of any healthy democracy. I also believe that young people are underrepresented in the political process, and would like to help provide a voice to this group.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have always been one to seek compromise and common ground between opposing sides, and intend to apply those skills to the disagreements that will inevitably unfold on the commission. As someone who has traveled to every county in Colorado, I am very familiar with how much the reality on the ground (economically, socially, culturally) varies greatly across the state, and how all voices must be heard and respected before decisions are made. Although I am a Democrat, I do not disparage those who have more conservative views than I do, nor do I assign labels to their beliefs or voting histories. Respect is a necessary precondition for fairness, am I pledge to bring that to the table every day.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As a science major, I have always used logic and reason to solve problems. In my professional work, I rely extensively upon U.S. Census Bureau data and mapping software such as GIS to do research and write reports. I’ve also learned to be a healthy skeptic of assertions that seem too good to be true, questioning whether there are perhaps some other relevant sources that should be consulted. In order to deliberate the answers to complex social questions like redistricting, all credible inputs need to be given full consideration, and I pledge to use logic and reason to sort through these inputs to achieve the fairest possible outcome.
