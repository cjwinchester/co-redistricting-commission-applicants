Statement

As a Millennial (32 years old) Coloradan, I want to set an example of what civic duty and engagement in a non-partisan setting looks like. I can't imagine a better opportunity than participating in something as important as redistricting- something that comes along once every decade. Lastly, I want to be part of a solution and not just more "sand in the gears". I know that this process has been very contentious in the past and often left to the courts to decide even up to almost a decade after the fact. I would be honored to be part of the inaugural process that we have pioneered in our state and hopefully serve as an example across of the country of what can be accomplished when Democrats, Independents and Republicans come together for the common good.
