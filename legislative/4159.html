Statement

I would like to serve on the commission not only to gain more political experience in Colorado but also to help make sure that moving forward from Donald Trump's presidency, we can guarantee the citizens of Colorado fair districts that will represent them fairly and make sure the peoples votes count. I would also like to be apart of this committee to have a clear voice for colorado natives and how we should be represented as the population of Denver, and the greater Colorado area are growing with new residents.

Through my experience in ASCSU, I was not only able to work on my impartiality in order to work with senators who did not share the same opinions as me but was able to attain focus and problem-solving skills which I feel would be of great use to the commission.
