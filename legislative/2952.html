Statement

I started a business in 2014 called Connected Realities. I have a strong belief in helping people tell their own stories, hear and understand other people, and find collaborative solutions that work for everyone. Part of what is underneath my desire to do business the way I do is the imperative that people be able to speak up and be heard. The way congressional and legislative districts are drawn can result in some voices not being heard adequately. I want to do what I can to make sure everyone gets a chance to be heard.

Statement on Working with Consensus

This might be my greatest value as a commission member. I am a consensus builder by nature and by profession. I can facilitate processes of helping people express hopes, fears, concerns, opportunities and needs AND getting people to hear and understand other people's needs and then work together to find solutions to meet the needs of the whole.
