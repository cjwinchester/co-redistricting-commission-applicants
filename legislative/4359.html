Statement

I want to serve on this commission to ensure the integrity of the voting process for all Colorado voters. I want every Coloradoan to feel that they are voting for individuals who represent their areas so their concerns and needs are front and center every election. Colorado is an incredibly easy state to vote in but these types of independent commissions will be integral to keeping voting safe and fair in the future.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Volunteering and working at schools in Boulder County has given me a chance to work with a variety of adults with different backgrounds, concerns, and different ways of coming to decisions. I have a deep respect for every individual's opinions and believe that listening to the concerns of everyone on the commission and giving people time and space to air their concerns and views is essential to come to fair decisions that will benefit all voters in Colorado. Sometimes groups need a person who can step back and suggest a different path to resolve a problem - I believe I can be that person for this commission.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I'm skilled at breaking down large problems or challenges into small sections to create a step by step plan of action. I think these commissions will need to be able to look at the issues at hand from a variety of angles to avoid.disagreements that can stall progress. Politics can be dominated by emotions, but redistricting should be dominated by facts. It is everyone's job on the commission to come to decisions that will be fair not only for the next election but also for future elections. An open mind is essential.
