Background

Professional Background

I have in addition to the political experience listed, been a Vice President of a small company (sales/marketing), a translator, a teacher, a public relations director, a Speakers Bureau Chairman, an advocate and a fundraiser.

Past Political Activity

I was elected to positions in college and grad school. I have been a registered Democrat for most of my life. I was briefly a Republican for the 2006 election. I was an active member of Florence City government from 2003-2007 (City Council, Planning Commission). I was the Treasurer(2007-2008) and Chairman(2007-2009) of the Fremont County Democrats from 2007-2009. I was the Democratic Candidate for Congress (2010, CO-5).

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I have not been a member of any group (except Colorado Democrats) since November 10, 2015. I volunteer in my community.

Statement

I am keenly interested in representation for all communities within Colorado. I participated in 2011, legislative and congressional redistricting in Pueblo and Salida. As a native, and resident for all but about 10 years of my life, been an avid traveler and politically aware Coloradan. I have worked in the government and private sectors, including for contractors to government contracts for the government (Minerals Management Service of the Department of Interior, Civil Division (Aviation and Admiralty and Toxic Torts) of the Department of Justice, and US State Department). Spanish and my love and frequent travel to Latin America are part of my understanding that may be valuable to the Spanish speaking community at home and abroad.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I am a Democrat in Republican dominated Fremont County, I live and breathe in the current 5th Congressional District: Chaffee, El Paso, Fremont, Park, Teller and I ran for office when Lake County was a part of it. I know Colorado, from the Four Corners, to Springfield, to Sterling to Dinosaur National Monument, five generations of my family call Colorado home. I am acutely aware of what it means to be a resident of Denver, and the rural beyond. I have lived in rural Fremont County longer than I have lived anywhere. I want a competitive division of districts that do not punish smaller counties.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have been a bookseller for more than 30 years. I read and sell a wide variety of books, all kinds, with just enough stock to offend everyone and anyone. I have friends across the world, country, state, county and city that are the scale of human endeavor. I work within deadlines and constraints. I believe learning is lifelong and value it deeply.
