Statement

In recent years, I have wanted to be a more active citizen. I believe giving one party an advantage via gerrymandering suppresses representation. An independent, citizen-led commission of voters from across the state will diminish the influence of politics from the redistricting process. Districts with similar legislative concerns can be created to provide cohesive representation for voters who have been marginalized through partisan redistricting. I want to be part of this first step to create fairer districts.

Statement on Working with Consensus

To create agreement among team members, I listen to what the facts are before asking clarifying questions. In an unemotional, non-judgmental way, I provide well thought out feedback. I prefer diplomacy, and logic. I am able to encourage team camaraderie because my personality compliments almost every personality style. I believe these traits give me the ability to be fair and impartial.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As a data steward, I collect data from various sources to create a database of required information. To determine what data is necessary, I gather information from multiple stakeholders. Using the information gathered from stakeholders, I determine what data will be useful and create visual reports for decision makers.
