Statement

I would like to promote transparency in the reapportionment process to promote public education and awareness about the importance of the redistricting process. I would also like to promote engagement with communities of interest to ensure community members are informed about the process and have a meaningful opportunity to participate in the drawing of the lines.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I would like to focus on encouraging open communication between diverse community groups and building opportunities for interested parties to reach agreements on final maps that will serve the public. I believe open and transparent communication and input throughout the process will be key to the success of redistricting process. As a student and young attorney, I was involved in political activities. I liked the process of being connected to my community, learning what impacted their lives, and analyzing problems to see if government was part of the solution. My professional life has required me to be fair and impartial in recommending government solutions.
