Background

Professional Background

When I lived in Colorado Springs in the early 1990s I volunteered for, and later was employed by, Citizen's Project. Since then, my organizing activities have focused on bringing the joy of dance and music--both participatory and in performance--to Colorado audiences, especially seniors. I organize dances, concerts, performances, and vintage fashion shows that brighten people's lives.

Past Political Activity

I've volunteered for the Chase Kohne 2018 District 4 campaign and I donated to that campaign. I've been a precinct leader for for several years. I donate monthly to Emily's List.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I am a board member and current Vice President of the Boulder Dance Coalition. I direct and perform with the dance troupe, Watch Your Step! Vintage Dance Vignettes, and I perform with Hora Romaneasca, a Romanian dance troupe, both of which perform for senior communities, festivals, and community activities.

Statement

While my volunteer activities to date have focused on the somewhat frivolous pursuit of spreading joy, now that I am retired I want to dig deeper into ensuring a just society where all votes are equal, and where Coloradans can trust that the results of the redistricting process are fair.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have years of professional experience facilitating meetings to achieve results that all team members can support. Each team member, no matter their background, has something to bring to the table. I believe that by hearing and debating all of the various points we can focus on a solution that works.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

In my career as a technical writer, my primary duty was to analyze scenarios and use logic and reason to create documentation that would allow someone to solve a problem, whether it was to understand a complex computer network or to use software and data to create a map or diagram. I am good at observing the status quo, drawing connections, identifying missing information or functionality in a system, looking for improvement possibilities, weighing the options, and focusing on a solution.
