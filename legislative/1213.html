Statement

I believe that the work of this commission is essential to ensuring fairness in the way in which our legislature is districted so that no group's interests are being held above any other's.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a former researcher and current medical social worker, I am faced with the necessity of impartiality and fairness every day. No one patient or client can be more important to me than another, and I am encouraged professionally and personally to constantly monitor my own biases and challenge them internally and in group/team discussions. I believe that honestly, frankly, and sometimes painfully addressing our own biases is the key to the change that is at the heart of this commission's work, and that is the perspective that I bring in order to promote consensus and agreement -- greater awareness of ourselves and, in turn, of each other.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have published research multiple times throughout my career, and very much enjoy approaching problems logically and analytically. My research has focused on sociol-emotional learning, substance use in young adults, PTSD and Depression, and severe mental illness. I am able to look past the emotionality of intense issues in order to see the underlying systemic causes and effects -- from substance abuse and mental health, to economics, to abortion.
