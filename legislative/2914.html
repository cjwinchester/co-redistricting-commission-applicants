Statement

As a career military officer, I have a strong commitment to honor and honesty. These commissions perform a vital role in our democracy. Having taught American national government, the Constitution, and the American political system as a faculty member of the US Air Force Academy, I feel well qualified and motivated to serve on this commission with an open mind and an honest intent.

Statement on Working with Consensus

My first military assignment was as a "finger on the button" nuclear missile launch officer. In that role I was charged with the direct defense of the United States. Later military assignments included AF Academy faculty, the White House (NSC TDY Staffer twice), as a military advisor at the State Department, and more. These assignments and my life experience have shown me the vital role the citizens of this nation must play, lest we slip into demagoguery. As a military officer, my job was to be politically neutral and to defend our national interests. Additionally, I served four years on the staff of US Senator Michael Bennet, handling military and veteran casework, military academy nominations, and more. That experience was very helpful in understanding the reach and the limits of congressional power to help our citizens. I am confident I can bring the same skills to the Commission.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My doctoral studies included a minor in what poli sci people call "methods." Simply put, that means I took a number of classes in advanced statistics and related study of the American electorate. My 17 years teaching at the AF Academy helped me to be able to clearly identify problems and to reach cooperative solutions. In addition to strong critical thinking and analytical skills, I have strong research skills, honed in doctoral studies and congressional work. I have strong communication skills and pride myself on being able to work with anyone, regardless of differences of opinion. I would be honored to work with the Commission, and I feel I would bring a great deal of useful experience and knowledge to the process.
