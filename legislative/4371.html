Statement

I am passionate about our democracy – the checks and balances, the rule of law, the guarantee of due process. These systematic protections are meant to ensure fundamental fairness as both the perception and existence of fairness are necessary to our democracy’s endurance. The mere perception of favoritism—bending the rules, trading on privilege—undermines our faith that the system is fair, eroding the foundation for our system of government. This independent process is designed precisely to restore and preserve that faith. These commissions have a tremendous opportunity to lead by example – to dispose of partisan politics and prove that those of differing political views can come together to evaluate information and data, deliberate and debate courses of action, and through an informed and open process come to consensus on critically important issues. It would be an absolute privilege and honor to serve the public as a commissioner.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Consensus is essential to nearly every successful endeavor, and a process that promotes informed deliberation is key. When stakeholders are heard and their opinions respected in a free and open exchange of ideas, it is easier to identify common ground or principles and then build from that foundation. My own management style is centered on collaboration and inclusion; I seek out views and opinions from every member of the team, regardless of seniority, and encourage healthy debate and active engagement by the entire team. I would bring this same approach to my work on the commission. I will be entirely fair and impartial and act only with the best interests of the electorate in mind.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Notwithstanding some very fine lawyer jokes to the contrary, lawyers really do have a knack for logic. My eighteen years in the profession, including trying and litigating high-stakes, complex commercial matters, have provided me an opportunity to hone and refine my analytical skills. I am very comfortable analyzing complex statistical and financial data and related information, distilling that information to identify meaningful insights and important trends, and applying those observations to the relevant law or rubric.
