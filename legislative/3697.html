Past Political Activity

Nothing since 2015, in-fact I resigned most of my positions in 2001. My first campaign was for Eugene McCarthy for President, then Shirley Chisholm for President, Wilbur Hobby for Governor of North Carolina, George McGovern for President, Jesse Jackson for President, Bill Clinton for President, Al Gore for President, during the entire time I was a precinct committee person, I served on several commissions and committees of the Colorado Democratic Party, the Central Committee of the Colorado Democratic Party and as Co-Chair of the Garfield County Democratic Party. Organization and Advocacy Experience

I was president of the Mountain Education Association, a CEA local. I served on the Board of Directors of the CEA for seven years, I was Chair of the Higher Education Advisory Council of the CEA, I served on the Resolutions Committee of the NEA and the Steering Committee of the Gay-Lesbian Caucus of the National Education Association. I was a member of the Teacher's Group, the first organization for Gay-Lesbian educators in the State of Colorado. I was the National Co-Director of Gay-Lesbian History Month during its last year as an independent organization. I served on the faculty advisory committee for the Colorado Department of Higher Education. I served on a number of Statewide Faculty Committees and Task Forces over the years. I was president of the not-for-profit Internet Service Provider, the Roaring Fork Internet Users Group.
