Statement

As a white, naturalized citizen of the USA I have never been challenged or had my right to be here questioned. I arrived in 1968 at the age of two, papers in order and can not for the life of me understand the animus against DACA participants or BIPOC communities. My work with the NAACP is a reflection of that. I advocate for the equality of all persons. As redistricting is at the heart of equity and inclusion in our political process, I hope to help ensure it based on data provided by the 2020 Census.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Growing up in the back of my grandmother's diner, I came to know a motley crew of regulars, ordinary - sometimes, extraordinary, people. How you treat them and how they treat you is everything. That and everyone loves hash browns made on the grill. Start there, and work your way up to the homemade chili crowd.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My ability to focus on the message, and not the delivery, necessarily, allows me to hear good ideas wherever they come from. I also have a healthy respect for un-messaged data and clearly delineated outcomes.
