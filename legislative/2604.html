Background

Professional Background

I have been involved at many levels from participating or providing support/information for community groups to being an organizer of meetings. I served for a couple years as the manager of the Upper Gunnison Water Conservancy District and in this role had to work with local, state and regional governmental and non-governmental groups. I have worked for the EPA and Forest Service so understand the roles of federal agency in working with state and local entities.

Past Political Activity

I have been mostly involved with local elections and campaigns. I donated to both county commissioner races this year - for Darien Wilson and Lisa Neal Graves. I have been involved with the school board elections in Douglas County and gave money to several of those campaigns.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I was part of Leadership Douglas County this past year. I am a member of the Colorado Bar Association and Douglas Elbert Bar Association. I am also a member of WealthCounsel. I have in the past been part of the Alliance of Professional Women. I have been on the social community in the past for my neighborhood HOA, the Benevolence Committee for my church, and worked on various committees for my children's schools. In the past 5 years I have not served on any formal boards.

Statement

I believe it is important to have a diverse range of view points and experiences on this commission that can fairly represent the people of Colorado and ensure that redistricting is done in a manner that best serves our citizens for years to come. Voting is a keystone of the democratic process, and now more then ever ensuring its integrity is important.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have held moderate views my entire life and have been able to maintain good relationships with a diverse group of friends and work with a wide range of diverse entities and view pointes. While I am not afraid to speak up with concerns or thoughts, I like to make sure everyone feels heard and understands the reasoning behind decisions.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I believe having a law degree and practicing as an attorney for the last 18 years gives me analytical skills and an understanding of the process of districting that are important. I also believe that being involved in positions involving lots of varied viewpoints and requiring decisions to be made and justified publicly provide beneficial experience.
