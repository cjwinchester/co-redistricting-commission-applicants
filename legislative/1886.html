Background

Professional Background

I was a steering committee member on The Cherry Creek Community Legislative Network (CCCLN), a non-partisan committee promoting awareness of education issues, legislation, and the legislative process. The Committee sponsored regular meetings and events to help community members learn about education issues within the district and legislative matters concerning public K-12 education in Colorado. The CCCLN provided opportunities to meet State Legislators, the Cherry Creek School Board, and other state officials. For about 4 years, I have been a co-leader for Indivisible Centennial West, this political group composed of about 400 members was created to take action in our community, build collective purpose, and create change.

Statement

I feel that how districts are drawn can shape a communities ability to elect officials that represent their unique interests and needs. It is my hope that this is done fairly so communities have equal access to political representation. Hopefully, the commission is formed in a way where a diverse population has a voice.

Statement on Working with Consensus

First, and foremost I will be a good listener and solicit ideas from others. I will stick to the facts so I remain fair and impartial. I will talk it through and always be honest.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

When up against a problem, I apply critical thinking by observing, being curious and objective but also identifying biases. I like to look at the whole picture and find the strengths and weaknesses.
