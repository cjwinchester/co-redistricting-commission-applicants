Organization and Advocacy Experience

As the Director of Community Development Director I work with a wide variety of diverse interests and stakeholders. In planning processes there are often differing ideologies and perspectives which as a planner, I must include, engage, and work with to develop community solutions. As part of my regular professional duties I work with the Gunnison County Board of Commissioners, Planning Commission, Environmental Health Board, and Sustainable Tourism and Outdoor Recreation Committee. All of these groups represent diverse stakeholders who are addressing complex problems--my work requires that I support collaboration of these diverse interests while I also helping them move toward practical solutions.

Statement

I want to serve on the commission to help represent the diverse interests in Colorado, especially those of communities on the western slope. I believe that democratic representation that accurately represents our diverse Colorado communities is foundational to successful democracy.

I am accustomed and skilled at building consensus, working collaboratively and cooperatively. As local government staff much of my work requires a fair and impartial approach to project review and community process. My work and professional standards demonstrate my ability to work across political, social and demographic divides to develop practical and collaborative solutions to complex challenges.
