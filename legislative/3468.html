Statement

The duties of my previous positions as County Clerk & Recorder and, thereafter, County Treasurer were clearly defined by the Colorado Constitution and we're not influenced by any outside group or individuals. I consider being a Member of this redistributing commission a similar position of public service...and not political in any way. The duty is to all the citizens of the State -conducted in an impartial and fair manner.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Consensus building can be achieved through listening to others and respecting their input.
