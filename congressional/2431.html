Past Political Activity

Each year I donate to a variety of candidates and political organizations. Each donation tends to be $50 - $100.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I volunteer as Treasurer for the Grove at Mesa Meadows HOA. I also volunteer as Treasurer for Animal Evac Volunteers, a member of the Jefferson County Animal Rescue Team. I am a volunteer trainer and site coordinator for AARP Tax-Aide, and I volunteer at the Action Center in Jefferson County doing data entry, working in the clothing bank, helping at special events, etc. I am a member of the League of Women Voters.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

As a Board Member for my HOA and for Animal Evac Volunteers, I help ensure that they are financially stable and that they meet their obligations to their members, to Jefferson County, and to the state. For Animal Evac Volunteers, I participate in community outreach events to talk to people about the importance of having an emergency plan and knowing the resources that they can call on during an evacuation. I also occasionally volunteer to represent the Action Center at outreach events. For AARP Tax-Aide, I help train volunteers and coordinate the Standley Lake Library site.

Statement

Our democracy requires district maps that are fair, not skewed to benefit one party over another. To create the fairest and most equitable maps possible, I think that voters must play a significant part in the process.

Statement on Working with Consensus

The goal of the commission should be to produce the fairest possible maps to serve voters for the next ten years. The commissioners might have varying ideas about how to do that, but I think that they can come to an agreement if they all keep the goal in mind. Throughout the meetings and hearings, I would use open-ended questions and follow-ups and a respectful attitude to help the commission get to a proposal that serves the voters.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My background is in business and supply chain management, so I am accustomed to analyzing information and considering alternatives. I know how to listen to varying perspectives before making a decision.
