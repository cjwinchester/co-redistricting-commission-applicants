Past Political Activity

I have attended social gatherings to meet local political candidates in my community, such as those running for county commissioner, school board, and the like. List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Local Chapter of Sierra Club involved in Fountain Creek cleanup, The Blocks Neighborhood Association, National Alliance on Mental Illness, East High School activities sponsored by PTSA.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I have many years of experience organizing and coordinating the efforts of groups that include professionals in healthcare management, public health officials, volunteers, and community members. In my most recent professional role, I engaged team members from local community mental health, public health, businesses, volunteer organizations, and service clubs to develop messaging, dissemination plan, and continually improve and refine a statewide campaign to reduce stigma associated with receiving mental health services. In addition, my role included data collection, development of an assessment tool, conducting the assessment, analysis of the results, development of plans to address identified gaps, and coordination/mobilization of teams to begin work to create solutions.

Statement

As a fourth generation Coloradan, I care deeply about our state and the people who live here. I want to be a part of smart decisions made by a thoughtful, conscientious group. I want to do whatever I can to make sure all our citizens' voices are heard. Every community in this state deserves fair maps.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I am good at promoting consensus by listening carefully and finding threads of agreement and pointing out common ground. I am fair and impartial fist by admitting that we all have biases whether we are aware of them or not, and then I work hard to overcome biases, look at issues from points of view that may not be my own, and come up with ways to innovate good solutions that benefit everyone. My ability to communicate technical information to any audience allows me to help others by building consensus and helping to mediate tense situations.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My skills lay in the ability to look at a problem in a logical way. When dealing with sticky social problems like outdated ways of thinking or lack of resources, I apply a step by step process to work on the problem from the ground up--knowing what I would like the outcome to be. By methodically using analytical tools such as logic models or other data analysis techniques, I know how to develop strategies for problem solving, develop plans, and execute those plans. Additionally, I have the ability to manage projects and make deadlines.
