Statement

As a Colorado native, I have a long and enduring passion for ensuring the best for this state. As someone with a political science background, I firmly believe reducing partisanship of our governmental institutions is key to ensuring U.S. democratic norms crucial to the functioning of our Democracy are maintained.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Due to my nearly 20 years of experience first as an US Air Force officer and now as a Department of Defense civilian, I understand the need to place personal political beliefs aside and remain apolitical in any role serving the public. During this time, I have received numerous courses on building team dynamics/cooperation, empowering others, and setting conditions for consensus. While serving in the US Air Force and now as a DoD civilian, I have nearly 20 years of experience promoting consensus with individuals with a wide spectrum of backgrounds and experience, to include years of experience working with foreign partners and allies. This allows me to respect differing viewpoints and backgrounds in order to achieve results championed by all.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am a certified intelligence analysis tradecraft instructor and educational course designer. This means that my federal agency has recognized me as someone who has completed the requisite training and possesses experience understanding cognitive biases, heuristics, and employing structured techniques designed to mitigate inherent biases while encouraging creative thought. As an intelligence analyst, I daily apply analytic skills, logic, and reasoning to complex problem sets in a non-biased and logical fashion.
