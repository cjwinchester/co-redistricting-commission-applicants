Organization and Advocacy Experience

Most recently, I have been most involved with the Colorado Alzheimer's Association serving as a volunteer ambassador to Congressman Mike Coffman, and, most recently, to Congressman Jason Crow. I also raise lots of money for the Association through the Walk to End Alzheimer's. As a Democratic PCP, I have held house parties for candidates, made phone calls to voters, canvassed, and make literature drops.

Statement

I have always been interested in how Congressional Districts are drawn. As a mathematics educator, I am interested in statistics and data. Also, as a state mathematics consultant for the Colorado Department of Education for 12+ years, I traveled around the state consulting with teachers, administrators, and parents and got to know the state well. Plus, I'm a Colorado native having been born in Greeley, grew up in Pueblo, went to college in Boulder, and have lived all my adult life in the Denver metro area (Aurora, Denver, and Centennial). I would find it very interesting to look at various areas of the state and see how best to draw boundaries that represent Colorado voters.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I would be sure to listen to all members of the commission and their viewpoints. I have facilitated many groups of adults in my professional life and I think these facilitation skills would be useful. Although I am a Democrat, I endeavor to understand the viewpoints of my friends which include many Republicans.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am a mathematician and an educator. I think my background in mathematics as well as my work experience as a classroom teacher as well as a state administrator uniquely qualify me to be a valuable member of this commission.
