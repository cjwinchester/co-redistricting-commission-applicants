Statement

I understand the history of how and why the legislature was created. I truly believe that we have lost the purpose of the foundation of it and are ignoring the guiding principles of “equal” representation. I feel the gerrymandering has granted the urban areas a vast amount of political parties and has completely left rural Colorado underrepresented and voiceless.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I feel that I can bring an understanding of the Great Compromise that was created by our founding fathers to why they chose to divide the legislature as they did. I will bring an understanding that urban and rural areas have great differences and needs, but that each need the opportunity to have a true voice in their government. I understand this is not to have one side “win” but to bring genuine representation and compromise.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am a K-12 teacher. Daily, I have to make many minute by minute decisions understanding every choice or decision that I make has consequences. I hold firm in my heart that facts must be heard, that emotions cannot be the driving force when it comes to decision making as hard as that is. I do not like to rush any decision, but every decision I make I try hard to be fair based on the information I am provided.
