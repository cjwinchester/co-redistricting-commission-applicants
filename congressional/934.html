Background

Past Political Activity

During the 2018 election I donated money to several candidates for Denver Mayor and Denver City Council races. In the mayoral race, I donated to Pennfield Tate and Lisa Calderon. In the city council races, I donated to Candi CeDebaca and Tony Pigford.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I am a member of a number of organizations, including New Hope Baptist Church, Colorado Morehouse Alumni club, Colorado Bar Association, Sam Cary Bar Association, and various committees through MSU Denver.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

Over the years, I have served on dozens of commissions and boards. I have been on Mayor and Governor advisory boards. I have been in leadership positions in numerous capacities as identified above. I have volunteered for political candidates and in those capacities I have worked with very a wide range of people in which I was able to motivate and organize.

Statement

I am interested in seeing districts drawn fairly and in a way that citizens will have their voice heard. I have studied the extensive partisan and racial gerrymandering that has gone on in our country. I just want to help ensure a fair and transparent process in helping to draw the congressional districts. I have a very mellow disposition and can help mediate situations when conflict arises. These are very tense times and it is more important than ever that "cooler heads prevail." I believe I am one of the cooler heads.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I provide legal commentary once a week on legal and political issues. Many of these issues have deep partisan divides. Despite my personal views, I believe my weekly segments are an example of how I can remain objective and see (and articulate) both sides of an issue. In my practice as an attorney, I have honed the skill of focusing on the facts and keeping my eye on the ball. In this capacity, I am most interested in ensuring fairness in the process. I will bring that energy and a calming demeanor as a commissioner.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am an attorney who has litigated many cases in front of juries and judges. Trial requires a clear, analytical presentation and analysis of the evidence. I am also a tenured professor at MSU Denver, where I teach Legal Environment of Business and Employment/Human Resources Law. Presenting complex information to students in a way they can comprehend and analyze it require the ability to apply logic and reason to a problem. This is the critical analysis that I teach my students. This is a skill I have acquired and will make me an asset to the commission.
