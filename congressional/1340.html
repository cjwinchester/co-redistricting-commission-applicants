Statement

With the exception of a run for our Board of Education 10 years ago, I have not served nor run to serve my community in any official capacity in some time. I learned of this opportunity at a recent Women's March and I decided to apply. My years of professional experience, and my prior years on our local board of education, along with my desire to "get back into things", will make me a good choice for this commission.

I was able to form good relationships with members of the board whose views were significantly different from my own. In my professional life I have served as mentor, supervisor, educator and trainer; based on feedback from my immediate supervisors I believe I have been fair and honest in my interactions with colleagues.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am good with people, with "service recovery" when that situation occasionally occurs, and with communicating important information in language each patient understands. I am passionate about many things, sometimes very vocal, but generally objective when it comes to addressing problems.
