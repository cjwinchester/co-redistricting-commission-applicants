Statement

I believe that gerrymandering is a serious issue throughout the USA as it unfairly dilutes the voting power of some citizen's and magnifies the voting power of others. In addition, I believe gerrymandering promotes extreme partisanship in the gerrymandered districts. I want to assist in ensuring that Colorado does not become subject to gerrymandering.

Statement on Working with Consensus

My last job before retiring was an Environmental Engineer with the Bureau of Land Management where we were required to take public comments and develop consensus on our various plans and decisions. In addition, the agency had a stakeholder process and management vetting that promoted fair and impartial decisions that were consistent with laws and regulations. I gained valuable experience in developing stakeholder consensus from that experience. More recently I have been a volunteer member of the Sustainable Wheat Ridge committee empowered by the Wheat Ridge City Council to prepare and coordinate implementation of the Wheat Ridge Environmental Sustainability Action Plan. This work involves consensus building among Wheat Ridge residents and the City Council.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission
