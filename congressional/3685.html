Statement

I am committed to seeing the full diversity of Colorado represented and treated equitably in as many political, civic and community spaces as I can. Redistricting is an area that can have tremendous impact and significant implications for the future of the marginalized populations of the state, and I believe diverse representation is critical to ensuring equity results from the process.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have spent my life and career building bridges among people of differing perspectives and opinions. I pride myself on being able to place myself in another person's position, and gain a sense of what is important to them, and why. I also understand the mechanics and importance of negotiation and compromise, always seeking to find a win-win solution whenever possible. I have typically been solely in a position of influence rather than one of power, which has helped me to hone my ability to promote agreement and consensus in multiple settings.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My career background is primarily as an IT project and program manager. I was able to craft an IT career from a Religion degree primarily because of my ability to analyze, reason, and problem solve logically. I have been involved in multiple high-profile and delicate situations in my career, including the Bank of America-Merrill Lynch merger in 2008; all of which have taught me skills in navigating complex situations and finding feasible solutions, even amidst difficult circumstances.
