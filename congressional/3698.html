Organization and Advocacy Experience

In retirement, I have not been as active in community organizing as in my past professional career. In my immediately prior nearly forty year career, primarily based from Washington, D.C., I have done extensive successful non-partisan community organizing for participation in political process in a number of States.

Statement

I have an extensive expert knowledge of political and legislative process. I have taught Ford Foundation-funded and community college seminars in same. I have a deep belief in a level playing field for our Democracy, citizen civic awareness and responsibility and an ardent sense of patriotism borne of love of my Country.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have a proven track record of achieving bi-partisan support from Members of the U.S. Congress evidenced by the successful equity in educational legislation that I have been the primary advocate for. This success derives from my proven ability to respect the differing points of view in achieving both Republican and Democrat co-sponsors of my legislative initiatives. In order to achieve success, my ability to compromise was also essential. It was impossible to enable legislation without bi-partisan support: I was 100% successful in every legislative endeavor I undertook over more than twenty-five years in advocating with the U.S. Congress.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Among my most successful endeavors has been achieving appropriations. In order to achieve these, it was necessary for me to conduct extensive research and analyses to justify appropriations requests to Staffs and Members of Congress. I have a proven successful track record in researching, analyzing and presenting relevant data. This kind of analysis also yields new and unexpected findings that an open mind is willing to seek the most equitable and fair answers, which I always did. My goals in such have always been for fairness, equity and a level playing field. Always guided by these ethics, solutions are always less difficult to arrive at. My formal education at the graduate level has also provided me skills in this arena. My 100% success in achieving data-driven/justified legislation and appropriations underlines my capabilities in this area.
