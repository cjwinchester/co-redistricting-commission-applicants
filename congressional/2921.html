I believe that I have the ability to quickly identify cause and effect relationships. I am reliable and a pragmatic thinker. I make decisions based on meaningful data and information. In my current role at Donut Holdings, Inc. DBA LaMar's Donuts, I am constantly using these steps to help solve issues. Why can’t it be done?
