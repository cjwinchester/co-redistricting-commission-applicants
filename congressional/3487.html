Statement

I understand how redistricting works and I want to be sure that the local communities and ALL populations are taken into consideration.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have worked for decades in bringing people from different cultures and political affiliations, together. I have held both elected and appointed positions at the local, regional, state and national levels, so I am well-versed in how policy is researched, analyzed and decided upon.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Immediately out of college I designed a one-page form for income verification which the State of Colorado's Office of Employment and Training adopted to use in order to cut through red tape, in 1979. I became Alamosa County's first ever Latino elected official in 1984. I ran against an incumbent Republican County Commissioner and won during Reagan's nation landslide election. I figured out how to do by analyzing the demographics of Alamosa County, working closely with the local media and reaching out to voters who had never supported a Latino candidate.
