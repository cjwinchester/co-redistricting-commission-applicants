Statement

As a student of world history, I have always believed very strongly in the virtue of representative government. Government 'by the people' can only succeed if ALL demographics are given a voice. Growing up in a rural county, I saw first hand how failure to secure local representation could effectively silence the voice and concerns of the minority opinion. We often felt disenfranchised and underappreciated. I believe the good of the whole is best served when all voices are heard and needs considered. As a mother of five children, this is played out in our home on a daily basis.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe the best way to approach any problem involving multiple opinions is to first come to an understanding of the position of all involved parties. Once this is established, then look for the common ground all views share. From this point of consensus, you then move forward with creative suggestions for compromise on the remaining areas of disagreement. The ultimate goal is not to have winners and losers, but to find the Win, Win for all involved. I believe there is always a way to creatively reach this goal if we are committed in the pursuit of it.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Analytical reasoning is a strong quality of mine and has been an essential skill developed over many years of successful small business ownership.
