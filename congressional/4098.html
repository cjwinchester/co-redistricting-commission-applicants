Organization and Advocacy Experience

I have participated in local and national volunteer committees for candidates and initiatives, including organizing phone banks, door-to-door information campaigns, candidate initiative debates, and neighborhood charettes. I have coordinated physical space for community volunteer phone banks for national elections. Generally, these campaigns revolve around voting rights and environmental protection.

Statement

I believe Colorado deserves to revisit its population distribution to accurately be represented at the national level.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As an active community member I am used to communicating with my neighbors and peers on issues that will have a direct and lasting impact on our lives, be it through taxes, development, or environmental protections. These are issues to which I am strongly committed, yet through conversation and education the best solutions are often the result of compromise. I know it is more important to gather consensus than to be "right".

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

For more than 25 years, my husband and I owned and operated a small business in our small town. We were always sure that our vision for our restaurant would see us through lean times and crises whether or not of our making. When faced with challenges, we were able to re-evaluate and adjust what worked and what didn't. These decisions were not always what we wanted to do, but what was best for our business and our community. Successful entrepreneurship requires flexibility, dispassionate and frank observations and clear-headed decisions while putting your personal brand into the public realm. I believe the ability to do this is one of my strengths.
