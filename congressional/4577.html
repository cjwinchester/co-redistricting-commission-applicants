Organization and Advocacy Experience

As a senior leader for the City and County of Denver at Denver Human Services it is my primary responsibility to ensure staff have what they need to better serve our community. This includes representing and advocating within our agency as well with our external partners including the Colorado State Human Services and Denver Juvenile Court to ensure our staff and the families they serve have all possible services and resources. As well, I am active in collaborating with City and Community agencies to address gaps in services and resources for the families served by Denver Human Services.

Statement

I am a native of Colorado and value the voice of the people when electing officials to office. I want to ensure that citizens of Colorado have equitable opportunity for their voices to be heard among all state representatives.

Statement on Working with Consensus

In my role at Denver Human Services, I oversee approximately 150 staff members and directly supervise 5 administrators and it is imperative to seek feedback in all major decisions to ensure staff commitment to any large system change. As a leader in a community service organization and as a trained social worker I value the voice of all impacted by any system change. I have skills in facilitating conversations to seek feedback and be solution focused in my questions, which guide individuals in finding solution to problems. I feel those skills will translate to the ability to build consensus and build agreement among commissioners. As a social worker, I have to approach each family Denver Human Services serves without judgement, in order to assist in creating long-term solutions for each family situation. As well, as a leader in my agency I have had to mediate staff conflict and have had to approach those situations with an impartial lens, in order to resolve the conflict. I believe those skills will translate to this process.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I oversee programming in Child Welfare which is Federally mandated and an Adult Protection Services program that is State mandated. As a result, we are required to be in compliance with measures set by the Federal and State government. I am well versed with using data to make decisions for my program and how to ensure we are meeting the needs of the clients we serve.
