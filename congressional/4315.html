Background

Past Political Activity

As the former Executive Director for One Colorado, I worked to elect pro-equality candidates to the state legislature. This includes managing c3, c4, PAC, and Small Donor Committees. I've given contributions to numerous Democratic candidates, including Joe Biden, David Cicilline, James Coleman, Jena Griswold, Chris Hinds, and Robin Kniech. I've also contributed to a number of advocacy organizations, including the National Center for Transgender Equality, The National LGBTQ Task Force, The Equality Federation, One Colorado, New Era Colorado, and ProgressNow Colorado.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Board Member - New Era Colorado, Board Member - Community Shares of Colorado, Fellow - Arcus Foundation's Executive Director Leadership Program, Commissioner - Colorado Supreme Court Nominating Commission, Trustee - Colorado Mesa University Board of Trustees, Member - Colorado Bar Association and Colorado Judicial Institute's Diversity on the Bench Initiative

Organization and Advocacy Experience

In my work at One Colorado, I built bipartisan, statewide campaigns to improve the lives of LGBTQ Coloradans and their families. As a statewide organization, we conducted a number of statewide tours ranging 13-18 cities to engage communities, solicit input, build strategic partnerships across Colorado. Through these conversations, I engaged with business owners, conservatives, faith leaders, parents, educators, and to help define and refine our efforts for LGBTQ equality.

Statement

As a young, gay Latino who grew up on the northeastern plains, I know what it's like to not feel like your voice is heard and you're not represented. I'm also the first one in my family to vote and now, as an advocate, understand the importance of making our democracy accessible so all can participate. This redistricting process is important to get "right." To do so, this process must be fair, impartial, engaging, and transparent in a way that earns the trust of the public.

Statement on Working with Consensus

In my roles as an advocate, the most important thing I could do was ask questions and be curious. All of this was done through building relationships with people to better understand their perspective. These are important qualities in bringing the commission together to deliver results.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As an advocate, my role was to understand problems, engage input, and explore solutions to address these issues.
