Statement

I'd like to serve on this commission to bring a diverse voice and perspective as a community member as service provider within the community. Understanding the import role that districting pays in political power, I want to bring voice to the community that I live and work in. This districting can provide opportunities for equity if done in a thoughtful and strategic manner, I hope to be a part of this in service to the better good of everyday people in the community. I also offer a distinct perspective through the work that I do, and understand how re-districting can mitigate or amplify community outcomes relating to violence.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I do not claim a party specifically to remain open to all views. While building a consensus is difficult work, I believe that through listening to the nuance of everyone's concerns, we can find common grounds when we may not see them, and we will have to compromise so that terms are broad enough for all parties to agree.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As a person that works to promote and instill healthy self-expression skills and healthy relationship behaviors in youth, I have to meet students where they're at, both culturally and academically. In order to reach young people, I practice dialoging in order to understand their strengths and the strengths of the communities they come from, so that I can advocate for health expressions and relationship dynamics in a way the compliments and does not contradict their self and their culture. There is an art to this and after 9 years with PAVE I have become pretty skillful, and I believe this skill set can be applied to this commission as well.
