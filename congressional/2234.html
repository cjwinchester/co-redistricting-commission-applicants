Statement

The redistricting process is important and the inclusion of voices representative of our state means the result represents the decision of all Coloradans. I think I could contribute positively to the process.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have the ability to discern fact from opinion and weigh the relevant facts in an impartial manner with the goal of making the best decision possible. When opinion is properly identified, narrowing down the relevant question makes it easier to reach a consensus.

As an attorney, I apply the facts of any client to the law and provide counsel regarding their legal options and the best path forward. The process requires the application of common sense, logic and reason as well as application of the law and ethical concerns.
