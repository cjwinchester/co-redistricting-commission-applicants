Statement

I am dedicated to the electoral/democratic process, in which redistricting plays a crucial role. I want to do my part to ensure a fair and equitable redistricting process so that maps are drawn to represent all Coloradans. I believe my experiences and skills in organizing, management, and civic engagement are well aligned to the tasks required of commissioners.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have completed training courses in consensus-building and have applied and honed those skills in my volunteer and professional experiences. As a legislative aide at the Colorado State Capitol, I collaborated in a bi-partisan fashion with fellow Democratic and Republican aides, staff members, and elected officials on bills. In my other jobs, I have worked in large groups which necessitates compromise, consensus, and fairness to advance organizational and personal goals. My four siblings and I were raised to value fairness and collegiality - otherwise, in a family that large, things can quickly devolve into chaos and hard-feelings. My upbringing has informed the work in my adult life, and I will bring that same inherent perspective to this commission.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have held a number of positions that required problem-solving and analytical skills. As the campaign manager for a candidate for state house, I worked with voter maps and lists to understand prevailing preferences and needs. Based on that information, I collaborated with the candidate, staff members, and volunteers to hone our message, canvass voters, and eventually win the election. Along the way, we encountered a number of barriers that required us to take a step back and map out the best solution and path forward. At New Era Colorado and EVmatch, I've continued to hone my analytical and problem-solving skills to improve organizational performance vis-a-vis teamwork. I am excited to bring these skills and experiences to work mapping congressional districts alongside my co-commissioners.
