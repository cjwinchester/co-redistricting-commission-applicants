Statement

I believe that it is crucial to our democracy and local governance that our local, state and national political representatives to accurately and fairly reflect the makeup of the districts they are elected to serve. Insuring that those districts, in turn, accurately reflect their populations is a key to that fair representation, and that's what these commissions are entrusted to do.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I feel that my background in education combined with my age makes me a good candidate to reflect the needs of some of those key constituencies. Over the years, my background in public relations and problem solving has made me a welcome and thoughtful member of any team or planning group to which I've been selected. I pride myself as being "a voice of reason."

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As noted above, I bring a reputation as being a thoughtful member of teams to which I've been appointed or on which I've served. I'm always willing to look at all sides of issues and willing to compromise and sort things through to reach a final result that works to a fair and equitable advantage for all.
