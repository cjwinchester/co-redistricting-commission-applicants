Statement

I believe the ability of all Coloradans to participate in our democracy is fundamentally tied to the ability of EACH Coloradan to have a meaningful vote. Partisan gerrymandering diminishes the ability of politically minority interests to have their voices hear and concerns addressed.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I'm a deal attorney. Fundamentally, my job is to build consensus and promote compromise to consummate a transaction. I have voted for candidates from both major political parties, as well as third parties, during my life and fundamentally believe that congressional districts should reflect common community, and not political, interests. My legal practice represents industries with widely varying political tendencies, from my oil and gas clients (who tend to be more conservative) to my green energy and technology clients (which tend to be more liberal).

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have extensive experience as a transactional attorney and a formal economic and philosophical education, with a focus on social and economic justice theory. My career has been based on my ability to use logic and analytics to bring disparate interests to agreement.
