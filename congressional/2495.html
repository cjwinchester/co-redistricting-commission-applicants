Statement

I think that each party jealously segregates the districts to favor their successful reelection goals. Versus looking at regions as communities with specific interests. To be advocated for equally. The urban centers disdain the rural and their dominance of the resources we each provide. This is creating the divergence into a virtual two state scenario. If there is no consideration for our common good, we will face the same disconnect already seen in other Urban vs Rural state disconnects. We need respect and consideration for both viewpoints.

I've been both viewpoints and know that if we do not diffuse the encroaching disruptive steamroller attitude by one party over the other we are virtually cutting off our nose to spite our face and our state looses the love both sides feel because of the hate that is encouraged by this inconsiderate lack of power sharing.
