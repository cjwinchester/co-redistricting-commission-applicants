Statement

I heard on Colorado Public Radio you need volunteers. I decided I can devote the time to it. I firmly believe in non-partisan districting, so that every Coloradan has as equal a voice in government as possible.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I am skilled at working with groups, have a lot of experience with various sized groups- from amateur clubs to professional groups. During my career I have been Executive Director of a professional trade association, where part of my duties were to "keep the peace" between fiercely competing members. I was quite successful at this, increasing memberships and income for the seven years I served in this professional capacity. I firmly believe in non-partisan congressional districting, so that Americans of any political philosophy can be equally represented.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am a relentlessly logical problem solver. Though my education major was creative writing I have ended up , during the 13 years in my second career in solar, working with numbers more than words. As an economic modeler I have built formulas and spreadsheets to combine different, disparate technologies, utility tariffs, billing analyses and numerous other factors into combined models. This is to optimize the economics of a project, and optimization of any project or program is vital to its success. First one must determine facts. Then recognize conflicts, predict future conflicts, and recognize the potential for negative ramifications for any particular solution, to get to the best way to move forward.
