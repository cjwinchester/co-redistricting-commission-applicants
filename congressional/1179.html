Statement on Working with Consensus

1) Ensure that everyone contributes and has their ideas heard. My ability to be impartial comes from a lifetime of working directly for elected officials and facilitating their discussions.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I analyze issues or processes by breaking it down into all the moving parts, and objectively assessing how those parts move and who is impacted. As I consider changes, I gather input from those who would be affected, asking specifically how the process is working now and what they would desire as a different process or result. For a problem (as compared to a process) I would brainstorm potential paths with others on my team, looking at the values or purpose rather than starting with a proposed solution. Once everyone fully considers the values and purpose, the solution will become clear.
