Statement

I understand the history of gerrymandering and how it creates a system where politicians select the voters instead of voters selecting their politicians. It is an unfair and toxic political manipulation for power and control rather than representing a populace.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe in collaborative systems instead of competitive set ups. Competition is a zero sum game where one side has to win and one side has to lose. Compromise sets up an atmosphere of whining that you didn't get what you feel you deserve. Collaboration brings everyone to the table to define the issues, combine possible solutions to collaborate on decisions where everyone benefits instead of the entitled or privileged minority with wealth, influence and corruption.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I understand how to look at problems from many different perspectives and positions. Problems are not solved effectively when special interests drive the conversation to serve narrow opportunities. It's time to consider a doughnut economic system serving people over profit, eliminate an electoral college component created to support slavery as an economic platform consolidating power to the few and provide an equitable system of one voter equals one vote. After 50 years of voting, it's time to look forward instead of backward. The "good old days" were on great for the "good old boys." It's time for this country to mature to include all Americans as citizens of a country that actually works for them rather than against them. This country was founded on white supremacy and it's time to own up to our real history and stop telling ourselves stories that degrade and demoralize people of color, women, non-binary folks, disabled communities and under privileged people who work 2-3 jobs or more to support their families. It's time to stop deluding ourselves with American exceptionalism and privilege. We can all do this together if we really want to.
