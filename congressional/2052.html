Statement

As a lifelong Independent voter I have never identified with a political party. I have thought and many years the key to avoiding excess political divisiveness and resulting stagnation in government is to create neutral, evidence-based, based districts. I am proud to live in a state that promotes these values with this commission and I expect to enthusiastically support the work of the commission whether or not I am chosen to participate as member.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have a mediation certificate from the State of Colorado mediation training. I served as an unofficial internal mediator for the Colorado state Public defenders from approximately 2002 to 2007 when I left the employment of the public defenders. In addition to being called back to the public defenders for some mediation work I have also done mediation work for the Colorado Department of Transportation. Further my work as a criminal defense attorney in the on the West Slope has required me to develop a more impartial orientation for negotiation success.
