Statement

In the final months of the 2018 election season, I made several presentations to the clients of Active Minds concerning the redistricting commissions. This rekindled my interest in the topic. During my 35 years as a teacher, I often discussed and commented on gerrymandering; serving on this commission would give me an opportunity to finally do something about it.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Getting a group of people to "row in the same direction" is what I did for most of my professional career. In addition to my many years as a high school teacher, coach and club moderator, I spent 28 years as a Naval Officer (4 active, 24 reserve.) I spent my adult life listening, building consensus, and achieving goals. I think I am well suited for the purpose of this commission.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

There are few tasks more challenging than developing a grading and evaluation system that is fair to students with widely varying skill sets. I spent my teaching career creating such systems. As a Naval Officer, I wrote and signed numerous performance evaluations. Those fitness reports directly impacted the careers of the individuals under my command. Fairness and impartiality are key elements in that system, and I upheld those principles throughout my career.
