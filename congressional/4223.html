Organization and Advocacy Experience

As a Colorado native, having lived in Mesa, Jefferson, Boulder, Denver and Arapahoe Counties, I have a unique perspective on the issues and concerns affecting Colorado citizens, regardless of geographical location and political affiliation. In particular, my service as Chair of the Colorado Hispanic Bar Association's Judicial Nominations and Endorsements Committee for five+ years has made me sensitive to the diverse political voices across the state. Similarly, my service on the University of Colorado Presidential Selections Committee has enhanced my appreciation of those political differences.
