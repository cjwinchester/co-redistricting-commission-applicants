Organization and Advocacy Experience

I lobbied on behalf of Recycle Colorado in the offices of Colorado legislators on both sides of the aisle, bringing up non-partisan issues of being eco-friendly while economically sustainable. My work as a mental health case manager in Denver gave me opportunities to lead therapy sessions and advocate for clients in the public sector.

Statement

I would relish the opportunity to participate in the process to ensure equal representation for all Coloradans. I have the time and energy to give to this project at this point in my life and would like to give back to the state I have enjoyed being a resident of for 23 years.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I am an analytical thinking with a close attention to detail and this would allow me to find common ground among commissioners. I don’t have any preconceived notions as to how the lines should be drawn and I am extremely open to hearing what the constituents believe should be done and am a very good listener to hear all the different sides of an issue. I look forward to learning the pros and cons of all sides and have an open mind.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Logic was always an academic strength of mine in school, as exemplified by my ACT score and Logic seminar grade in my senior year at Vassar College. I will apply logic and reasonable approaches to finding compromise among my colleagues.
