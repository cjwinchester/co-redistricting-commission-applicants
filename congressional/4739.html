Organization and Advocacy Experience

As a member of Localworks I worked with committees to promote the city of Wheat Ridge as a vibrant place to live. That work began in 2012 and ended in 2018. As a member of the Neighborhood Revitalization committee we had to meet with members of the community and collect ideas from all factions and present our findings to city staff and council as to what direction the citizens wanted Wheat Ridge to become. I have always, since high school, been involved in collaborative efforts to bring about change or improve something. Usually as the leader of the group. (Show up, speak up, and listen. Statement

Because I believe that districting should be fairly divided so that both parties have representation. Districts should not favor one party over another.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have Steven Covey training, seek first to understand and then be understood. I don’t desire to be in charge of anything or push any agenda. I have led lots of groups as a facilitator and can help groups stay focused and on track. I can teach people how to stand on their head, so I can also explain a process or concept and help people overcome fear And I can lead a group in breathing exercises to help calm everyone down!

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I also worked in instructional design prior to being a technical writer, so from that I learned to ask a lot of questions.
