I have worked in a variety of careers (possibly too many!) where I have used conflict resolution skills and practice impartiatility and fairness. My education created a strong foundation in learning how to separate facts from emotions to best analyze and understand information in an impartial manner. This was then further cemented through career experiences that required the ability to be fair or impartial from my role assisting Veterans with VA claims to leading operations for a non-profit focused on social entrepreneurship.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As discussed above, my educational experiences created a strong foundation for this skillset - I have a background in research and statistics. Moreover, when working as a Director of Operations at a small start-up it was necessary to apply logic and reason to problems ranging from roommate issues to budgetary issues.
