Statement

I believe in fair representation for all people. This issue is (or should be) not about political beliefs, but about representation of the people and our democracy working for all the citizens regardless of political affiliation or geographic location.

Statement on Working with Consensus

One of the most useful skills I have learned is to listen to several points of view and distill them down to the essential framework of the issue. I believe this to be the crux of this task. As far as fairness and impartiality is concerned, I have had to practice fairness and being non-judgemental in the classroom and with many patients at the medical practice. The most basic respect is the respect of others to hold their own opinions no matter how different they may be from my own. My political impartiality comes from my Philosophy background. This particular endeavor speaks directly to my belief that politics may change, but finding the consistent universality of a system that can provide the framework for those politics is essential. This task is much bigger than the current political climate that is fleeting.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My Philosophy degree gave me the skills to see the abstract. My business experience gave me the skills to practically apply ideas and strategies. My teaching experience gave me the skills to compassionately communicate.
