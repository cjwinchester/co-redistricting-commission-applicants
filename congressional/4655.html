Statement

Redistricting is one of the most important aspects of our democracy. Unfair gerrymandering of districts has eroded voters faith in the system and the value of their vote. Districts should be drawn to reflect the citizens therein, not the political outcome leaders may want.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I worked for 2 years for the Office of Legislative Legal Service for the Colorado General Assembly which is a non-partisan office. Even if you have strong political beliefs, as I do, it is perfectly possible to work in a non-partisan manner with people from all political parties. Redistricting should be non-partisan, so working with others is key to make sure that districts are reflective of all citizens.
