Statement

I believe in the democratic process and I believe that this process is currently broken down by partisan politics. Too often dominant groups have the loudest voice at the table and protect their interests. I want to be an advocate for those who have not been included in these conversations.

Statement on Working with Consensus

In my role at the state I had to navigate community needs and the desires of those in power. This required creative engagement and discussions to find a suitable middle ground.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I work on several projects that require analysis of data and application to programs. I also have experience synthesizing complicated concepts and making them understandable and applicable for community based organizations. I have experience with systems-level thinking/macro thinking that connects to the individual experience. For example, I served as the co-investigator on a randomized control trial that gathered data from youth across Colorado and evaluated a youth-suicide prevention program for several violence outcomes like sexual violence, bullying, harassment, suicide, as well as protective factors. I can use these skills to support this project.
