Background

Professional Background

In addition to working with political campaigns each election cycle, I have spent significant time in the last ten years volunteering in the local legal community. I am currently finishing up my term as President of the 9,000+ member Denver Bar Association which involves a number of different roles representing the membership, leading our board, managing the organization's programing and finances, and representing the DBA in the larger Colorado Bar Association and as part of the Presidents' Diversity Council that advocates on behalf of all of the local bar communities (e.g. CHBA, CWBA, Sam Cary, LGBT Bar, SABA, APABA).

Past Political Activity

2020 Elections - contributions to Alexis King for Jeff Co DA and Theresa Greenfield for Senate (IA) 2018 Elections - outreach leader HD4 for Phil Weiser's AG campaign; donated to AG Weiser campaign; served as Democratic delegate to district and state assemblies. 2016 Elections - volunteer for Michael Carrigan for Denver DA campaign; donated to the Carrigan for DA campaign; served as Democratic delegate to district assembly.

Statement

I have always been very civically engaged, but have volunteered my time and energy primarily in the local legal community and helping with the occasional political campaign. I became interested in more direct civil engagement with the redistricting commission after attending a program about the origins of the commission and its function. I was so impressed with this commission system that I wanted to get involved directly in this important work.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As someone who has chaired the boards for several large volunteer organizations and served as part of the greater coalition of the Presidents' Diversity Council , I have a great deal of experience with collaborative problem solving.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My undergraduate education as a duel Economics and History major provided me strong critical thinking skills which I further honed in law school. As a result, I am highly analytical even for an attorney and have the additional perspective of engaging in data driven decision-making. Based on this background, I regularly take on issues that involve the types of detailed analysis and understanding of finance that many attorneys avoid. My current work as an appellate specialist also makes use of these skills because my work focuses on legal and factual analysis in cases before the Colorado Court of Appeals and Supreme Court.
