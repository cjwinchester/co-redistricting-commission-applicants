Political donations to individual candidates or PACs <$200 since 2015. I'm a precinct organizer in #19 in La Plata county.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Most of my civic and community activities, which included serving on the Board of Directors for the Southwest Colorado Mental Health Center, and Chamber of Commerce was prior to 2015. Post 2015, I've been active in my neighborhood as a precinct organizer.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

As a volunteer in the 2020 general election, I organized phone banking, texting, virtual training and coordination of volunteers within our county Democratic party. As previously stated, I'm an advocate for public safety and mental health as well as small business. Prior to the Colorado statewide face mask mandate, I completed the John Hopkins Contact Tracing Course and advocated face mask mandates at the Bayfield Town Hall, Ignacio Town Hall, and the La Plata County Board of Commissioners.

Statement

As a proponent and contributor to a large voter turnout in La Plata county, I believe that voter participation is critical in selecting our leaders on local, statewide and national levels. My concern, in the recent election, was although there was exceptional voter participation by all parties, the outcome in CD#3 was heavily slanted to one party. I'm interested in analyzing and addressing an equal distribution of voters within districts to ensure fair competitions in elections, and will pledge to work towards that end in connection with all party affiliations

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe my communication skills and analytical background will prove beneficial in facilitating public input. Listening to public testimony will provide valuable input in order to reach recommendations that may not always reach consensus, but will serve the greater good in providing equality and fairness in our election process. Impartiality is key to arriving at unbiased decisions that will ultimately be approved by the court. And, a sense of humor is always helpful.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As a commercial lender for over 30 years, I analyzed tax returns, financial statements and projections to reach lending decisions, either under my loan authority, at senior loan committee, of which I was a member, or appeal. In analyzing our county voter demographic, I crafted a strategy that garnered between 95% and 90% Dem voter turnout in La Plata county, on September and October 2020 registrations, respectively.
