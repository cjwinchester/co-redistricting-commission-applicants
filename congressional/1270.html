Statement

I feel my party/political perspective is under-represented within state and federal elected positions. Colorado is very close to 50/50 Democrat/Republican however our state and federal representation is very imbalanced. I believe bringing our elected representation into balance would also help eliminate legislators that only represent their party as it would force them to work across the aisle to accomplish anything. If a single party doesn't have a clear majority, then every bill becomes bipartisan or it can't pass.
