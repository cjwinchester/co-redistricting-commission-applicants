Background

Past Political Activity

I have been a candidate for Mayor for Commerce City in 2019, City City Council at large for Commerce City in 2015. I have been a candidate for HD32 in Adams in 2016. I have donated to Dominick Moreno for Senate 21.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I served as a board of directors for North Metro Chamber of Commerce, Hispanic Chamber Foundation, Founded Commerce City Chamber of Commerce, Founded and chaired Adelante Community Development, board member of Commerce City Business and Professionals, board member for Belle Creek Charter School, board member for Community Enterprise, member of Our Lady Mother of the Church. Currently appointed to the Colorado Minority Business Board and Adams County Business Taskforce Development and Underinsured taskforce. I am currently an advisor for the Adams County Regional taskforce.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I have been a community advocate for the past 25 years. I have worked with driver licenses for all bills, working families issues for minimum wage pay, DACA outreach and education, prochoice women issues, census outreach, currently with COVID-19 providing education and resources with the community. I have been involved with Good Business Colorado and Small Business Majority to further equal business education and outreach. I am a graduate of the following leadership programs: Latino Leadership Institute at DU, The Hispanic Chamber of Colorado and the North Metro Chamber of Commerce.

Statement

Because Latinos and minorities have traditionally been unrepresented in this work. I want to make sure that as a Latina leader of my community I carry a strong voice of what are the pressing issues impacting our community. I want to intentionally be a resource for the overall mission of this group and future planning.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe in representation and fairness. However, I will not going to sacrifice communities of interest for the sake of reaching consensus. I will always hold true to my values and will not be at the expense of communities of interest.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I rely on data and proper analysis to be able and make logic. I believe in listening sessions, focus groups and common sense to understand the community needs. I am objective when deciding the opportunities in front us. Data is important to decide on solutions. I believe in representing a common ethic identity and a common language. Therefore, keeping areas together and provide the support needed is a priority for me.
