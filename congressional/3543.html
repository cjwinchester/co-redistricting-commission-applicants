Statement

I moved to Colorado in 1988 after graduating from college and finally felt like I had arrived "home." This state is full of natural beauty and I am constantly discovering new areas of that fill me with joy and gratitude that I live here. I want a chance to give back to the state and the people here who I see as mostly independent thinkers who can see multiple views on a variety of issues. The Colorado population has increased so much since I arrived that I want to help current and future Coloradans have their opinions heard.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a nurse I have patients and co-workers who are of all political persuasions. When the focus is someone's health it doesn't matter what political party he/she belongs to, the goal is still the same. The ability to listen, to accept people where they are in that moment but to also work towards a common goal is something I have developed during my time in the hospital as well as the outpatient setting.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Critical thinking is essential to nursing and evidence based practice is the foundation of good health care. With the information available to people online, being able to look at information and see if it is based in fact or opinion is crucial.
