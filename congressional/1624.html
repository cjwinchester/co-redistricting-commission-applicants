Statement

Very simply: I believe that this commission will be a force for fairness, strengthening our Democracy generally and for our Great State, specifically. I have the time and expertise to bring people to together with fairness, discipline and mutual respect.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have had extensive experience bringing disparate groups together—in fact groups that were vigorous competitors—when having to Integrate different management teams in three different large ($2, $4 and $10 billion transactions during my 16 years in different Coors entities) transactions. The most important elements in these efforts were to 1) listen intently to others’ points of view; 2) engage and excite others in generating new, value-added ideas, 3) always asking the exciting and important question, “what can we achieve together that we otherwise couldn’t achieve apart ?” With our country, and even to some extent our State of Colorado, so divided, I believe with all my energy that we have to do a much better job advancing and promoting listening to and compromising with those with whom we initially may not agree.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

CFO of two major Fortune 250 companies; 40 years of intense work at every level in Corporate Strategy, Business Planning, Strategic Planning, Financial Analysis, Investor Relations, Human Resource advancement and planning, Diversity and Inclusion; 14 years on the Xcel Energy Board (one of the 10 largest utilities in the US, $39 billion market cap), including the Audit Committee and Chairing that Committee for more than 5 years; Operations, Nuclear, Environment, Safety Committee, Finance Committee.
