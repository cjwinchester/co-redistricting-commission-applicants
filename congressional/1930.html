Statement on Working with Consensus

My neighborhood is a mixed neighborhood of full time residents and second homeowners. We have retirees and young families residing here. Two years ago, the Weston Pass fire was very close to us, and we were all concerned that it would impact our neighborhood. Every year during the three summer holidays we have young people riding dirt bikes and atvs in the neighborhood because we share a border with the USFS. That summer we were stopping people and verifying that those vehicles were not fire hazards. This summer at our annual association meeting, the riders were brought up again. Once it was pointed out that our state was just getting out of lockdown and everyone was ready to leave that time behind, the neighbors agreed that letting people have fun outside is a good thing. We had had spring moisture to keep the fire danger low for early summer, and the activity was only for 3 weekends out of the year.
