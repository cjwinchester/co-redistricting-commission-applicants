Professional Background

I am an active member of Zonta International and have been since I retired in 2008. I joined the Zonta Club of Fairfax, Virginia at the same time. I was elected to the Board of Directors for two terms and to the position of Treasurer for one term. I transferred my membership to the Zonta Club of Denver II soon after I moved to Colorado in 2014 and served on the Board of Directors. Zonta is a professional women’s organization dedicated to the empowerment of women the world over. Individual clubs donate a percentage of the money they garner from charitable events that they either put on themselves of take part in to Zonta International. Then the clubs use the remainder of their funds to local projects. The Zonta Club of Denver II supports the Florence Crittendon High School, The Women’s Homeless Initiative, SafeHouse, Empowerment/Women at Lowery, Teacher Grants to elementary school teachers, and other events by other charitable organizations that need extra help. I usually provide part of the dinners for 25 women in support of the Women’s Homeless Initiative six times a year.

Statement

I think gerrymandering is harmful to the political life of our country. I think this is a good way to stop it.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I was a software project manager for about 15 years. This included cost projections for various projects, scheduling work, analyzing the customer’s problems, designing and then implementing the solution, and managing the members of my teams. Over that time I managed many projects. Most of the projects were started at the original analysis and design stage and proceeded to the first iteration of the final product. I did not manage maintenance projects. I often had the beginnings of the project personnel with me during this first stage of a project. It was my policy to listen to the ideas of everyone on the project and come to a consensus of the best way to solve the customer’s problem. Sometimes my ideas were implemented and sometimes someone else’s ideas were better. We negotiated among ourselves until a consensus was reached.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Over the years of my career I took classes in analysis and design of computer systems. I then applied what I learned to my work efforts.
