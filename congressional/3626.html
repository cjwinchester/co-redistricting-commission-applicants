Statement

I feel very strongly about representation. Too often important voices in our community are left out of the conversation, and their needs are not addressed. I also feel that redistricting efforts, on both sides of the party spectrum, have too often been aimed at protecting entrenched interests through gerrymandering. I feel this has led not only to the silencing of many voices in our communities, but also to deepening partisan division. I deeply hope that the next redistricting can be done in a way that truly reflects the diverse interests of our communities. Also, to clarify, I was unclear on the qualifications for this position. My understanding reading the qualifications was that I needed to have been a registered Democrat for five years. I changed my party affiliation from Independent to Democrat in 2018 and therefore did not think I qualified. (I had been an Independent since I moved to Colorado in the late 1990s, though I voted Democrat.) However, the person who suggested I apply said she thought it meant I had to either be either a Democrat or Unaffiliated for 5 year, as long as I was registered. I am not sure that's the case but thought I would fill out the application anyhow, just in case, but understand if I am not qualified for this reason.
