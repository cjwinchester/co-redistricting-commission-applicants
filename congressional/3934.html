Organization and Advocacy Experience

I have assisted many of the above organizations in dealing with issues of governance, including the amending and restating the bylaws or other internal statutes of Littleton Town Hall Arts Center, Colorado Society of CPAs, COCPA Educational Foundation, International Hearing Dog, Colorado Bar Association, Aurora Bar Association and USA Fencing. Statement

Bring fairness and balance to the election landscape of Colorado so that our Congressional delegation mirrors the makeup of the population of the state.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have experience in bringing compromise and consensus when amending and restating the governance documents of various domestic and international organizations. I have always worked to create a fair and balanced process so that the majority will govern, while the rights of the minority are protected. I served as an arbitrator and mediator for AAA and COMA for approximately 40 years and practiced being fair and impartial, listening to all sides before rendering or assisting parties in making decisions. I am a true believer in creating a process that will serve no matter who becomes the majority at any point in time.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am a logic-based thinker. Every decision has multiple consequences and one must take each of them through many layers to ensure that as few unintended consequences as possible occur at any level. I constantly create scenarios as decisions are being made to make sure that the decisions will produce only the anticipated results. When there are outliers, it is necessary to continue to refine the process.
