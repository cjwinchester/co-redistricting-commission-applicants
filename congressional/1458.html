Statement on Working with Consensus

Because of my experience in sales, I believe I have learned to decipher people's concerns. I believe once you get to the heart of a concern, there are always ways to work through problems to find fair solutions. In the summer of 2016, I served on a jury for a murder trial. I was elected as the foreman of the jury. During our deliberation process, I was able to facilitate discussion from all of the jurors. There were three jurors who were strongly opposed from the other nine jurors. Through the discussion, we all came to the same conclusion. We did this by starting at what we could all agree on and then building from there. We all had to be fair, work within the law and be open with each other. I believe I helped us get general consensus much faster because of the open environment that I helped to create.This is a good example of how I like to work with others. Having an open atmosphere where all feel comfortable discussing opposing opinions is crucial in the decision-making process.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Because of my background in operations, I have an ability to work through a process. I take things one step at a time until I find why something isn't working. I find that I approach all problems this way. At one time, CEO Operating Inc. had 65 contractors. I managed the employees as well as the customers we served. I was successful in coming up with improved operations upon which everyone could agree. There was a time when my company was responsible to four different production companies. We moved millions of barrels of water each year with very few incidents. In order to accomplish this feat, I had to work with different personalities and ideas.
