Background

Past Political Activity

I am very politically aware and have donated to lots of political campaigns since 2015. Most recently, I donated to Democratic Senate candidates in Iowa, Georgia and Montana. I also donated to Amy Padden's DA race. I donated to Elizabeth Warren in the Democratic primary. In Colorado, I've donated to Julie Gonzalez, Faith Winter and Brittany Petterson.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I'm on the board of America's Voice, and immigration reform advocacy group based in DC. I'm a single mom of a 4 year old, so I haven't been very active in the community for the last few years.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I spent many years in my career advocating for immigrants and their civic participation. I have ties over the past 10 years to immigrant and Latino leaders and communities in Colorado who are striving for greater participation and representation in Colorado.

Statement

I believe this is a once in a lifetime chance to participate in the democratic process. I think gerrymandering has contributed to divisions and corruption in our political system and in Colorado we've really gotten it right. I value what we're building here and I think it's a model for the rest of the country.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have a lot of experience working in coalitions and helping groups get aligned. I've done a lot of group facilitation and really value making sure everyone's voice is heard. I understand that drawing maps in Colorado will bring out a lot of competing interests and I look forward to finding compromises everyone can live with.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

In my current work, I spend a lot of time analyzing policy, various perspectives on solving a problem, and then working with a team to execute on that. I would look forward to learning from the experts about what the criteria are that's needed to draw the maps and making sure we create the fairest plan possible.
