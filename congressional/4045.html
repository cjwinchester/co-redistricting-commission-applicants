Past Political Activity

I have donated to most major presidential and senate campaigns since 2008. I knocked on doors for John Kerry's campaign in 2004. During 2016 I donated to Clinton's campaign; during 2019-2020, I donated to ActBlue generally, and the Biden campaign, the Hickenlooper campaign.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I have experience advocating for Public Counsel of the Rockies, which hired me in 2005 to litigate issues related to water quantity and coalbed methane development. I was an expert witness on water law issues for the Colorado Democrats in the congressional redistricting case in 2011. Otherwise my advocacy has been on behalf of public or private water rights holders in the states of Colorado, Idaho, Wyoming, and New Mexico

Statement

I believe strongly in the mission fo the commission, and want to be involved in developing its policies and protocols as we move into what is hopefully a new era of less-politicized redistricting efforts. This is a way for me to give back to my community.

Statement on Working with Consensus

My law practice has taught me that the best route is almost always to identify and pursue what someone can do--not what they can't do. In that regard I have developed important skills in creative problem solving. Most of my most difficult cases have settled, even though there was plenty to be gained from litigation, but it is the settlements that my clients have been most happy with and of which I am most proud. As far as being impartial, my work also includes the role of judicial officer for the TROA parties which requires impartial decision-making. I also helped raise four children, which required significant efforts to be thoughtful and impartial in doling out both rewards and judgments--something our children would attest to I'm sure.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My day-to-day requires analytical skills and logic to resolve legal issues facing my clients. As manager of White & Jankowski, I also was faced with managing a budget, HR issues, and other business-related challenges. During my time as managing partner we always turned a profit, and the top earning years of the firm up until 2018 were during my tenure.
