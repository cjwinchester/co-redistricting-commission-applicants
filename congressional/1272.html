Statement

The redistricting process is essential to the fair allocation of voting interests in the state based on periodic shifts in demographic factors. An independent commission with a broad range of views can hopefully meet this assignment in a fair and effective manner.

Statement on Working with Consensus

In each of the positions in which I've served as noted above, I have had to help resolve disparate views on various subjects coming before the organizations in question. Sometimes those views have been widely divergent. I have found that preparing by research, including speaking with others, is critical in helping to reach a conclusion which, if not necessarily satisfactory to all, is understandable to all participants. Because I am willing to try to understand all points of view, including those different from mine, I believe I have the ability to be fair and fact-based in reaching a conclusion.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As a practicing lawyer, I was trained to apply analysis, logic and reason to resolving a problem within the context of laws. My history of experience in the legal profession and in business has helped me understand how to work within organizations with a diversity of views on any given subject and to give weight to all perspectives and reach a conclusion that most in the group can accept.
