Background

Professional Background

I spent my career advocating for the interests of the City and County of Denver and Denver International Airport. Now that I am retired, I am passionate about serving my community in the organizations listed in this application.

Past Political Activity

I have volunteered for GOTV efforts occasionally since 1976. I have donated money for various Democratic candidates since 2015, including Michael Bennet, Hillary Clinton, Joe Neguse, Mark Kelly, John Hickenlooper, and Joe Biden.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

First Unitarian Church - Building Renovation Committee, Music and Property Management Committees, Chair of Finance Council. Denver Bicycle Touring Club - President since 2019, member of Board of Directors since 2016. Osher Life Long Learning Institute at Denver University - member of Member Relations Committee since 2019. Denver Museum of Nature and Science - volunteer since 2015. Columbine Chorale, member since 2004.

Statement

I've had a life-long interest in public service since majoring in Government at Lawrence University, and continuing through law school at CU Boulder and through serving in the City Attorney's Office from 1987-2014. My service in representing the Denver City Council from 2000-2004, the Denver Board of Ethics from 2000-2014, and in serving on the Colorado Supreme Court Standing Committee on Rules of Professional Conduct all further highlight my interest in democratic process and the working of government.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I've had significant experience and interest in mediating and consensus-building: some significant examples include representing City Council as Deputy City Attorney from 2000-2004, supervising Airport Legal Services at Denver International Airport, representing the airport on the Denver Airport-Airlines Relations Committee, serving as chair of Project 01-15 (Assessing Airport Revenue Strategies) of the Transportation Research Board, National Academies, and serving as chair of the Legal Affairs Steering Group of Airports Council International from 2012-2014.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I spent all of my career with the Department of Law for Denver using my legal skills in solving complex problems during the construction of the New Airport, representing the Airport in bond transactions, supervising attorneys and staff, and assisting many boards and commissions during my career and also my retirement years in the areas of law, ethics, finance, and non-profit management.
