Past Political Activity

I have been engaged in political campaigns since I was 17 years old. I was precinct captain for years and navigating varying local political committees as well. I have donated funds to various democratic campaigns and/or donated my time to democratic campaigns as well for folks such as Rhonda Fields, Jennifer Bacon, Gail Pough, James Coleman and beyond.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I run a nonprofit organization entitled Young Aspiring Americans for Social and Political Activism (YAASPA) and founded it in 2010. We walk students through youth participatory action research to support them in bridging their ideas into policy and civic spaces. We also host student led events for local elections as well via nonpartisan lens. I have been a member of various professional organizations as well such as: American Education Research Association, Public Administration Theory Network, Association for the Study of Higher Education, Association Society for Public Administration and beyond.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I am connected to many amazing organizations in Colorado's ecosystem that we either partner with via my nonprofit or that I support as an individual. I am associated with and/or support Project VOYCE, 9 to 5 Colorado, Padres y Jovenes Unidos, Colorado's People Alliance, Aurora NAACP, Colorado Jobs for Justice, Colorado Criminal Justice Reform Coalition.

Statement

I want to ensure there is not simply compositional diversity, but also intellectual diversity as well. I hope to serve on the commission as I am very invested in Colorado as my home state and as someone who is raising children here, and building the next generation of civic leaders and public administrators. I am committed to ensuring equity is embedded in the process and in the dialogue.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I will always lean into curiosity and clarification questions in order to build bridges and deepen relationships and consensus building.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am a practitioner, but also a researcher and educator who has taught civics, political science, public administration, and ethnic studies. I am able to bridge theory into practice in these civic spaces.
