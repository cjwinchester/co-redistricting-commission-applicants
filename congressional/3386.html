Organization and Advocacy Experience

I have not joined in any of the political organizations in Colorado as I do not feel they represent me. But I would like to advocate for voters who are like me and are not represented by either political party. I feel all voters deserve a voice and I would like to know that those who do not feel that they are represented are heard.

Statement

I want to help make sure that there is no gerrymandering in the State of Colorado and all citizens have their voices heard. Every citizen has a right to fair and equal representation in government.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a long time independent, I hold no no party above the other. I think that the people with the best ideas are the ones who should be elected. We should be allowed to vote for the Best of the Best, not the lesser of two evils. I feel that gerrymandering doesn't allow for true competition that would hopefully bring out the Best of the Best. I feel I would not be swayed by party affiliation. I truly feel that democracy is an amazing form of government, especially when it works. For it to work everyone must be allowed to take part. All voices must be heard. We don't always have to agree, but we don't have to fight. We can discuss and talk through our problems. I feel when one side takes advantage and doesn't allow for fair and equal access to government, then democracy has failed. I would really like to help democracy strive and grow strong again.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As a small business owner I had to deal with emergencies and problems on a daily basis. You really learn how to think on your feet when you own a Cafe. You also learn how to talk to and deal with people. I am detail oriented, have good communication skills, highly organized and very logic based. I like to collect as much information as I can from multiple sources before making an informed decision. I feel that having lots information from multiple sources is key to good decision making. Also I feel that getting information from many perspectives is very important.
