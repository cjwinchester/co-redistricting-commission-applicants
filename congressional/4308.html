Statement

Since I was old enough to vote, I have continued to see change in this country. There has been change for the better and change for the worse. I want to serve on this commission for one very important reason. To make every individual voice count. Too often, partisan efforts to redraw districting lines has had a greater negative effect on our voting than a positive one. To be short, Gerrymandering has effectively silenced voices and suppressed votes. This idea that "my vote won't make a difference" is not something a democracy should see. Every vote should count. District lines should not be drawn for political advantage, rather to ensure that each individual feels as thought their voice is heard and have adequate representation.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Promoting consensus has nothing to do with political party. I approach problems with logic. By openly communicating, actively listening, understanding perspectives, and showing respect, we will be able to have a deeper understanding of commissioners points of view. Logic alone won't win over anyone. You must tie in the human factor (emotion/empathy). Topics exist on paper. Too often, they remain on paper as people bicker back and forth. By introducing the human factor, you are able to bring the topics to life. There is no wrong or right. There exist only ideas. If I am able to be successful on this front, then I believe that I can successfully promote consensus. In regards to being fair or impartial, I do not and will not consider myself above my own beliefs indicated above. It is about collectively working together. I intend to practice what I preach and put myself in the position of others. That is the only way to remain impartial. All ideas deserve the light of day.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I went to school to study Finance. A lot what I learned and what I do are analytical in nature. When I study the health of a company there are so many aspects to consider (present day cash flows, future cash flows, macroeconomic/microeconomic factors). These are analytical tools that I have to work with daily and apply in my profession. Putting thoughts on paper in this application is one thing, but talking to someone and being personable is another. I hope for the opportunity to speak and the honor to serve on this commission.
