Statement

For too long districts have been drawn to protect the major political parties without consideration of the diverse populations in those districts. While it is impossible to draw districts of "one mind", more consideration must be given to voters views to promote candidates that truly reflect those views.

Statement on Working with Consensus

It is important to understand each commissioners reasons and desires for their preferences. With this understanding I will lead to compromise and eventual agreement in an impartial way. As the makeup of the commision will necessarily be diverse in opinion and background, I will seek to find first the areas of agreement before working to resolve disagreements. By defining our agreements and then our common goals, we can then address those issues on which we need to compromise.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Throughout my career I have been responsible for many other peoples lives and livelihoods. I have always clearly defined success first, as in what goal are we working to achieve. Success of this commision will be a fair and equitable redistricting where voters will feel that they have a true voice and their chosen candidate will have a realistic chance at victory. To achieve such a lofty goal, an analysis of voter tallies, as well as stack-holders throughout the state will serve as a guide. This huge amount of information will then be reduced to dominating factors.
