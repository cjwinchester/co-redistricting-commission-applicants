Background

Past Political Activity

I am a proud native from a family of Coloradans who have supported democratic candidates and issues over many generations. I have knocked on doors and made phone calls to get out the vote for Joe Biden, Hillary Clinton, Tammy Story, Lisa Cutler, and Jesse Danielson during the 2016, 2018 and 2020 elections. I made campaign donations to Diane Mitsch Bush, Joe, Biden, Beto O'Rourke, Jon Ossoff, Hillary Clinton, and Jesse Danielson in the 2016, 2018 and 2020 elections.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I served as the Vice President of our neighborhood (Woods of Evergreen) Homeowners Association in 2019-2020 and on the Evergreen Middle School Accountability Committee in 2019-2020. I participate sporadically with the Evergreen Alliance for Sustainability. I sit on the ASHRAE (American Society of Heating, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Engineers) Epidemic Task Force for Commercial Buildings.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I served as the Executive Director of the U.S. Green Building Council Colorado Chapter, a volunteer-based organization focused on collaboration, education, advocacy and outreach in order to enable the design, construction and operation of sustainable developments in Colorado. On behalf of this organization, I worked across Colorado with community members, state and local agencies, consultants, students and trade associations in support of sustainable development principles, policies and practices. These stakeholders did not always align on messaging and philosophy, however we were able to build a middle ground, based on common interests, in order to identify and support organizational direction and action amongst the Board of Directors and members.

Statement

I believe that voting maps within Colorado should be guided by local and/or regional interests wherever possible including issues of climate, population growth, business requirements, economic potential and land and water use. As a Colorado native, I have watched the collective Coloradan philosophy evolve with growth and changing demographics. I am deeply committed to and rooted in Colorado and seek to make this a place that my kids will also be proud to call home. Redistricting needs to ensure that the voices of all Colorado residents are heard equitably, fairly and in a meaningful way so that our future balances the needs of a very diverse constituency, geography and economy.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a public servant I strive, and am committed, to fairly represent a multitude of stakeholders, relevant interests and taxpayers from all of the states and territories. I have found that people are passionate, diligent, committed, sometimes angry and impolite, but we are all human. Connections and compromise between humans can be achieved through an understanding of each individual's story and needs. This means using plain language, direct communication, exceptional and active listening, transparency and sincerity about your own issues, and keeping an open minded to the needs of others in order to find and meet in the middle.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I was trained to organize and structure solutions to complex questions and problems using evidence-and science- based metrics so that outcomes and solutions can be analyzed and evaluated using quantifiable data. This training was consistent through engineering and law school, and has been essential to show and support individual and organizational successes.
