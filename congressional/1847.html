Statement

My life experience is the driving force behind my desire for a more participatory democracy. Growing up in a low-income family with very humble beginnings, I’ve experienced firsthand the struggles of disenfranchised voters who feel their voice is being heard by those who were elected to serve them. Through redistricting efforts, we can ensure that elected officials do not become complacent in their districts, and in return, fiercely advocate for public policies that better reflect the beliefs and values of their constituents.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I truly believe that throughout our history, America’s greatest legislative achievements can be attributed to a thoughtful exchange of ideas, compromise, and an understanding that our country is at its best when we work together. Throughout my professional career I have led countless efforts to build consensus around complicated issues among diverse stakeholders. From healthcare advocacy across rural Iowa and dozens of rulemaking meetings to develop rules and regulations for Colorado’s marijuana industry, to working with statewide issue committees to draft language for ballot initiatives - I’ve relied on empathy, honesty and transparency to ensure fair and unbiased contributions to the efforts I’ve been a part of. These qualities have resulted in several nominations to leadership roles within numerous civic organizations.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

To advance political campaigns and public policy initiatives, I’ve relied on polling data, intense research, intel and pragmatism throughout my career to be successful. Analyzing demographics, voter turnout and other data points provide a greater understanding of the political landscapes I’ve operated in, and in return, assisted with the development of robust strategies that include tight messaging, fundraising goals, targeting parameters that increase the quality and quantity of each contact, and more. On the public policy front, I regularly rely on data to make compelling arguments in favor of legislative proposals. This means researching and understanding legislative impacts, notable opposition, budgetary concerns, potential concessions and forthcoming amendments.
