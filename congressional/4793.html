Statement on Working with Consensus

I live in a communal house with 6 other housemates and my husband. Suffice to say, I've learned how to communicate and listen to others from different backgrounds with different perspectives. I'm good at seeing an issue from all sides and then trying to find the agreement in the middle. As a more centrist Democrat, I also don't have knee-jerk reactions to ideas or policies that might be contrary to mine. Republicans and independents are good people and shouldn't be vilified just because we disagree about policy. I think the more diverse a group is in both backgrounds and ideology, the more likely that the final consensus will be fair and balanced.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As an engineer, I think my analytical skills are likely more developed than the majority of other applicants. My personality is such that I'm never the type to make decisions based on emotions. I'm also great with an Excel spreadsheet!
