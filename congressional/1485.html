Organization and Advocacy Experience

I organized a group called PVREA Action Group that has been working since 2018 to get our rural electric association (PVREA) to provide more renewable energy. We’ve done this by circulating petitions, attending board meetings, and managing a campaign for the Board of Directors. We have also worked with other groups to encourage Tri-State G&T to rework its contracts with state REAs, close their coal mines, and to provide a much greater percentage of renewable energy, in support of the state of Colorado’s goals to significantly reduce greenhouse gas emissions. In this capacity, I have attended numerous board meetings (of both PVREA and Tri-State), testified before the Colorado Public Utilities Commission, and organized/participated in petition drives. In 2019 I managed a campaign to elect a ‘green’ candidate to the Board of Directors of PVREA. That work involved overseeing all aspects of the campaign, from fund raising to setting up meet & greets and purchasing flyers etc., to organizing and leading weekly meetings with our leadership team from December of 2019 until the election in April of 2020.

Statement

Aside from wanting to serve my community and state, and to fulfill what I consider to be my civic duty, I want to do my part to make sure that the partisan gerrymandering that has plagued my natal state of Texas doesn’t plague my adopted - and much beloved - state of Colorado.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe wholeheartedly in democracy. While I may not agree with someone from the other side of the political spectrum, I passionately support the right of every American to have a vote, and for that vote to be counted. My love of our Democratic foundation, along with a deep sense of justice for all, make it extremely uncomfortable, if not impossible, for me to act in a way that is unfair or impartial in situations such as this.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I spent my career as an oboist and an oboe teacher. In essence, the job is problem solving. Whether finding a way to perfect difficulty musical passages for my own performances, or identifying hurdles in my students’ performances and finding ways to clear their paths to success, my career of 30+ years involved analyzing, diagnosing, and prescribing methodologies to affect positive outcomes. In addition, my experience teaching and serving on university faculties has taught me skills in communication, negotiation, and collaboration that are universally applicable.
