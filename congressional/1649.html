Statement

I believe in fairness and I voted for the creation of the redistricting commissions. I am a political moderate and I feel that the two main political parties do not fully represent my interests. I do not think I am alone in that feeling. I have never made a political donation before this year. My donations were all driven by the behavior of a candidate in standing up for the rule of law against hypocrisy or conspiracy theories. I am fair and balanced. I am used to working with people from all sides and working toward consensus. I like to spend time with people holding different opinions and I always look for the points on which we can all agree. I am skilled in diffusing tense situations and I believe that skill could be useful here.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have extensive experience listening to multiple sides and working towards consensus while trying to keep everyone talking to one another. I have served as president of my condo association in Chicago and mediated disputes, I have served as treasurer and president of a private school parents' association and pushed through an evidence-based increase in member dues to ensure lasting financial solvency for the organization. I am an excellent listener and I keep an open mind. I make decisions based on facts and data, not on feelings. My goal is for everyone to respect each other, even when we disagree.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have a law degree, I worked as a lawyer for years, and I recently graduated from graduate school where I excelled in data analysis. I love research and digging into the details.
