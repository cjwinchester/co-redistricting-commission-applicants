Statement

I live in a new neighborhood in West Arvada (Zip 80007) that does not really have proper representation at the City, State or even National level. Our neighborhood is new (community of Leyden Rock and Candelas) and I believe that we need a voice at the decision making table. I am also a women of color who is bilingual (Spanish) and a proud Latina. I believe that we need to diversify Commissions so that they include the people they are hoping to serve. Lastly I am a small business owner who works from home (Small Psychology private practice providing services to injured workers in Colorado WWW.CO-Assessment.com) and I believe that I represent a demographic of self-employed small business owners whose voice need to be taken into consideration. Dr. Massa-Carroll

Statement on Working with Consensus

I am a Doctor in Psychology, so I am trained to listen and de-escalate situations. Sometimes well-intended individuals approach controversial topics with a degree of passion that hinders a rational conversation. I think that my role is to intervene on those situations in order to promote an amicable , honest and fruitful discussion and achieve the intended outcomes.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have a Ph.D. so I have formal training on how to gather data, understand statistics (effect sizes, margin of error, sampling sizes , etc) assess the quality of the data presented and make decision accordingly. I have an excellent training as an Ivy League graduate. I am also Puerto Rican and I do not shy away from sharing my point of view on these matters. So I will be an active participate in problem solving and digesting the data in a way that can be understood by others.
