Background

Past Political Activity

As a city manager and a member of the International City/County Management Association (ICMA), I am bound by a Code of Ethics. Tenet 7 of the Code of Ethics prohibits political activity. In accordance with the ICMA Code of Ethics, I have not donated to campaigns or political organizations.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Organization and Advocacy Experience

As a former city manager and city attorney for several Colorado communities, I have spent my career working for the best interests of all residents in my communities. This includes the creation of task forces and working groups to create public policy as well as make recommendations regarding enhanced public participation in civic life.

Statement

When I go to the ICMA annual conference in cities across the nation, there is always a gathering of Colorado city managers, people from every part of our state. I have served at an international level in my professional organization and have met folks from around the world - and yet, in that one gathering , when I see my colleagues from Durango, Montrose, Pueblo, Sterling, Greeley, Aurora, Fort Collins . . ., I know that Colorado is a leader in government excellence. I love these people and want to be of service to them to promote good government in our nation. The voices of Coloradans should be heard in our nation's halls of justice.

Statement on Working with Consensus

One of the best parts about being a city manager has been the opportunity to sit outside the political realm and yet be a part of creating excellent public policy that will serve the residents of my community. I have done so many times - in creating a marijuana advisory commission; an elections review commission; a public participation working group; a capital improvement working group, a police oversight task force, all in Boulder. In my former city of Dublin, OH, I was the impetus behind the creation of a committee of elected and appointed officials along the US 33 corridor - people who never even spoke to each other before I brought them to the table.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

In local government, there is never a clear answer to the complex and wicked problems that we face. During my career, I have been repeatedly called upon to make decisions based on data and logic, as well as on the art of discerning the best interests of those whose voices are not always at the table. Since I am a lawyer and practiced law for 25 years, I believe that I bring a mind of legal analysis to every problem that I face.
