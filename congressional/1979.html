Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

Through my education and long professional experience (46 years as a self employed musician and teacher), I believe I have strong analytical skills and am able to apply logic and reason to problems. Anytime one is involved in making group decisions it is best to depend on logic and reason so that emotions do not overrun the process of finding solutions. It is also good to LISTEN to everyone before making decisions.
