Statement

The Commission allows for an excellent opportunity for public involvement and transparency as we shape boundaries at the Legislative and Congressional level. As a local school board member, consensus building based on presented facts using analytical skills, critical thinking skills and communication skills is done daily as we create policy, negotiate contracts, interpret data, to prioritize short and long term goals for our district, students and staff. I believe I have a unique skill set especially listening and consensus building skills with an emphasis on team work to best serve on the commission.

The most important attribute to building consensus is to be a good listener. Listen to all opinions and concerns both positive and negative. Analyze all data to to help set priorities and problem solve issues that arise. The most effective way to to present fairness and be impartial is to promote ideas based on accuracy of information and facts.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

We use use our critical thinking skills to look read the data that allows us to set the budget, restructure goals and set priorities. We use communication skills to build consensus and communicate with our parents, students and residents.
