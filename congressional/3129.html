Organization and Advocacy Experience

My work with the Presbytery of Denver and with Wellshire Presbyterian Church reflect this. I have worked closely in both areas to help the members develop a more global vision. There was very little active work in this area before I started working on it. I have also participated strongly in developing racial and ethnic visions at the Presbytery level helping to organize racial and ethnic groups. This is in addition to my work for statewide groups and foundations. Most of the time it takes the form of evaluation research, facilitation and training.

Statement

When we voted on Y &Z, I was excited that Colorado chose to develop a neutral Commission to design the new maps. So I was thrilled to receive an invitation to apply for this Commission. I believe I have useful talents for the Commission. In my work as President of the REFT Institute, a social research organization, I have traveled all over Colorado to gather input on various issues as a professional researcher. Also I am a professional facilitator, trainer and article reviewer and writer..

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a professor, my job has been to help students explore various different perspectives on issues. As a social researcher, I have to provide a neutral perspective and report only what the data say. I have facilitated discussion groups encouraging people with different perspectives to speak up and participate in developing a resolution. I have used this at the state level, in the classroom, and also at the national level where I headed up the Advisory Committee on Social Witness Policy which developed social policies for the national Presbyterian church. Part of my work was to convince commissioners with very different perspectives to support the policies the committee developed in consultation with people around the nation.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have taught research methods for over 20 years. That is based on the use of logic and reason to interpret data. That is also the foundation of a social research company that I led for 15 years. Currently I have served as a volunteer critiquing various aspects of the Arapahoe County's Democratic Party and candidates' materials in an effort to help improve the quality of the material.
