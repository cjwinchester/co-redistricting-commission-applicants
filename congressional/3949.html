List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I was an Elementary Principal for 14 years and during that time I was responsible for training, hiring, strategic planning, budget decisions, grant writing and supervising 50+ staff members yearly. I have facilitated districtwide trainings, participated in public forums and debates as a candidate.

Statement

Our democracy depends on fair and competitive voting maps. It is our jobs to make sure that our congressional districts are competitive as that will provide the best representation for the people who live in there.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have worked through my career to develop consensus and decisions based on facts and data. Once consensus is decided, I will work tirelessly to make sure it is implemented and successful. As I said before my goal is to develop congressional districts that are as competitive as possible and represent the public not only in a general election, but also in primary decisions as well.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As a science devotee, decisions need to be made based on the best available data and facts. I am committed to delving into the problem, unpacking the mess, asking questions that can lead to relevant and productive conversations. I am fully committed to the best, fair, and balanced decision based on the available facts and data.
