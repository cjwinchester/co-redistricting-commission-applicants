Statement

Precisely because I have not participated in any organizing or advocacy work. My professional career has pivoted away from direct work in lobbying & elections and I believe I am called to some degree of civic participation. Beyond that, I've seen how both parties have drawn districts in other states in ways that are patently unfair to voters. I voted in favor of this initiative because I believe in a process that draws fair boundaries & I believe I'm able to positively contribute to that work.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a negotiator, organizer, and project manager, my career has always required me working together with diverse groups of people to find consensus. I believe I have developed some degree of skill at identifying common ground & major issues. As a labor representative, I was required to be fair and impartial and judge situations based on cold logic & contractual language instead of emotion. I've applied that experience as a juror & believe that I can apply the same impartiality here. Again, I believe in fair districts that group communities together in logical manners, not districts that wind around in geometrically crazy shapes to obtain partisan political gain for any party.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As a software engineer, I apply logic and reasons to problems all day long. I write algorithms that solve problems and create applications that address business needs. I believe these skills, along with my aforementioned commitment to impartiality and communication experience, will serve me well in this capacity.
