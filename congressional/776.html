I regularly look to make sure that a point of view which I believe may not have been a factor in framing policy is presented to the right audience with data to support the perspective where available.

Statement

I believe truly independent and non-partisan redistricting is the best way for citizens with similar community needs to elect a government that will best represent that population. I also believe making districts which focus on shared demographics can lead to more open conversation among neighbors about what is needed in their communities and how to best meet those needs.

Statement on Working with Consensus

By being an independent voice whose primary interest is creating even, non-partisan districts for an electorate with similar demographics, I would look to even out any partisan arguments. I also professionally serve in a function where it is my job to find a reasonable path forward with multiple strong-willed individuals on a regular basis, an area where I’ve demonstrated success. My passion for this role is to help create district boundaries that will lead to the best possible consensus of its constituents, which I don’t believe has any correlation to a particular partisan affiliation.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I've worked with census and BLS data since I was in undergraduate studies for differing economic analytic & modeling purposes, which I continue to do regularly. I have database architecture & querying skills I've used for the majority of my career, as well as data visualization skills. The success I’ve had professionally has mainly come from my ability to use my analytical abilities to find efficient solutions to difficult problems, and then communicate my process and solutions in a way that is understandable to those with a variety of other backgrounds. Understanding the data more often leads to an agreed a path forward.
