Statement

As a native Coloradoan, I have appreciated that our state has been fairly evenly represented in the last decade or so. I want to make sure that all Coloradoans feel that they are being represented. I grew up in the mountains, and now live in the front range, so I understand the differences just over the divide. I am very passionate about fairness and compromise, and want everyone to feel that they have a voice. I have a lot of the skills that I think would be beneficial on this committee including GIS, data management, data visualization, and organizational management.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As I mentioned above, I have lived in several areas throughout the state, and understand that the needs of every community are not the same. I have lived in cities, as well as small mountain towns that rely on tourism. I understand the balance between environmental protection and the fact that our industries are found in those environments. I believe that having this larger perspective would serve me well in contributing to a fair and consensual outcome.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I work with large amounts of data daily and have extensive experience with spatial information as well as organizing projects and the needs of people. I believe myself to be very logical, and again, want everyone to feel heard. I am very excited that Colorado has taken on this approach to drawing the lines to ensure that every voice is heard even understanding the rules and limitations of the written law.
