Statement

As an Asian American I have for my entire life been sensitive to minority rights. Because the Asian American community is rather small in Denver, I believe it is important for me to make connections with all different minority communities. As a public defender, I had to be sensitive to the poor and the minority communities of north Aurora, groups of people I feel honored and humbled to represent.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a lawyer, it was an important skill to listen to my clients and get the complete story to understand what the issues were. Not every case required a trial. Many cases require an artful resolution, which would be befitting of the circumstances. In those situations, I would have to find common ground between what a City Attorney or District Attorney, victim and my client. I would have to work out a resolution that my client could live with and which worked to bring my clients to a greater understanding of his actions and how they impacted the victim and the community.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My entire professional career was based on analyzing a situation and law so that judges and juries reached the conclusion I felt it merited.
