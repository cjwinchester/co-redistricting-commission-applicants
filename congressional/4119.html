Statement

I believe that partisan redistricting is detrimental to the democratic process, and I am excited about Colorado's citizen-led redistricting effort. I'm eager to contribute to this effort so that it can prove its value and serve as a model for other states. I currently have time to devote to giving the commission my energy and enthusiasm, and I am looking for a way to give back to the wider community.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe that the path to consensus lies with respect. Commissioners who can listen to -- and respect -- one another will do the hard work of seeking mutually satisfactory agreements. I pride myself on my ability to listen and to work with others productively, regardless of differences in opinion. Although my views lean Democratic, I am not partisan, and I want to support processes that give a fair voice to all Coloradans.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As an editor, I have a keen eye for detail, and I know the power of words to shape a narrative and to clarify the logical flow of an argument. I am methodical and sensitive in my application of logic and reason when presented with challenges, and, most important, I always believe that a solution is possible with sufficient effort.
