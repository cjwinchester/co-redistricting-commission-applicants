Organization and Advocacy Experience

I am primarily active in interests that affect families and individuals who seek services from Jefferson County Human Services. I was asked to chair the Health Alliance this year. The Jefferson County Health Alliance is a collaborative, cross-sector partnership committed to a connected, thriving Jefferson County, where health and opportunity are possible for all. The Alliance takes a community-based approach to how health and wellness are defined in Jefferson County throughout the lifespan. It works across multiple sectors to promote principles of health, equity, social determinants of health, multi-sector collaboration, accountability, dignity and respect.

Statement

I believe fairly crafted legislative and congressional districts create the foundation for, and enable more robust debate about, candidacies and policy development for the people of Colorado.

Statement on Working with Consensus

In my current and prior professional roles I must work with individuals representing a wide variety of perspectives and opinions. Over my 20+ years in policy development I have learned to listen, to consider, to find common ground, to compromise. These experiences have developed my perspective: that utilizing these skills will lead to a better overall outcome when as many competing perspectives are aired, as possible.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

While I do not participate in the traditional practice of law, I am trained as an attorney where the foundation of that education is the development of analytical skills and logical reasoning. I apply these concepts every day, both professionally in various leadership positions, and personally. I am able to navigate leadership needs across a 700-employee organization in a servant-leadership model, by listening and thinking critically, asking questions. Personally, I have teenagers. This presents often challenging interactions. Seeking to understand their perspectives and lenses (where the prefrontal cortexes are not yet fully developed) has presented an opportunity to further hone logic and reasoning skills. Understanding the lenses through which others look when approaching disagreements and challenges assist in navigating a path to common ground.
