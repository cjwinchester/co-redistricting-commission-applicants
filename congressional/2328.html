Statement

I would like to serve on this commission to bring the voting districts of this state up to date with our changing demographics. I grew up in Colorado Springs, moved away for a decade to live in east coast cities, and now live and work in a rural school district. I've been witness to the shifts in population in this state, and would value the chance to deliberate with other Coloradans over what changes should be made and why.

Statement on Working with Consensus

We have all been witness to a lack of ability to work toward consensus from the highest levels of government to our own neighborhoods in recent years. One of my greatest talents is listening; while I enjoy formulating and defending my own opinions, I value learning about the life experiences and insights that come from others who have come to differing opinions than mine. Negotiation is a dying art, but I am in favor of resurrecting its use in all spheres, public and private.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have spent the last several years in school and in my work analyzing data and research for my profession and for course work. Some of the most engaging course work I have been privy to in the past year and a half is critical qualitative and quantitative analysis. I know that there is always more to a story or research piece than numbers present. The ability to turn a critical eye to context and what discourse is being left out of any discussion has become a passion of mine in my work and problem-solving in general.
