Statement

I think it is an important part of keeping politics fair and effective. As well as ensuring we can and do represent all Coloradans. I think it is important to ensure the vote has an accurate account of Colorado.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe in the value of everyone's opinion. I am a democrat but I grew up in a very conservative, republican area and I am familiar with their concerns, many which I feel are valid. I think there is a way to make government work for everyone, and to make sure no one feels as though their voice is not heard or not important. I have always considered myself to be a fair person, I like to find the reasoning behind those who disagree with me. In truth, I don't feel like you can accurately describe your opinion without understanding the opinions of others. I think I generally get a long with most people and I don't think I would have any problem getting along with the group as a whole.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I consider myself a logical person, I think its best to look at any problem from every angle. I enjoy finding ways to improve and enhance areas of difficulty. I ran a camp for people with special needs, and if you are not logical and rational in that situation it would be chaos. I think most issues are best resolved if you can take your emotions out of it, not your passion, but your emotions.
