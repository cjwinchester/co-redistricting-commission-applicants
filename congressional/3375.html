Statement

My professional and community service in education, human services, and legal improvement areas provide a balanced background for working with a team on redistricting issues. As an attorney (38 years) practicing in diverse areas of both public and private sector law and in a variety of forums and contexts, I am comfortable and enjoy interacting with people - hearing their stories and interests and helping to interpret systems with a goal of finding a concensus on what works.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Both as a mediator and a retired litigator, my objective is to help people identify the value (this is not necessarily economic) in their interests and to assist them in building a resolution that serves all in some way. As a mediator, litigator and board member in sometimes very heated discussions I find a way to help identify ways to evaluate sometimes diverse perispectives and find some unity. The best outcome is reached when all parties contribute to the process of dialogue and find some shared values.
