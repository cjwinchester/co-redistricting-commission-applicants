Statement on Working with Consensus

The beauty of multi-systemic teams is that people come together to offer diverse experiences and opinions. I was a Facilitator of Team Decision Making meetings for residents of Colorado, which included various stake holders, for eight years. I saw the power of coming up with plans, with the goal of reaching a consensus. Often times, not everyone agreed, but we ended the meetings with a plan that the involved parties could live with. My responsibility was to ensure that everyone felt heard. I will contribute on a commission by respecting other's opinions but ensuring that I give my opinion when, the the floor is open for me to do so. In my current position and for the past 12+ years, in my various roles, I have demonstrated that I can be fair and impartial.
