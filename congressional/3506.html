Statement

Gerrymandering is a huge issue in politics that makes it harder for fair elections to occur and stifles the voices of many people, in particular people of color. I want to make sure our elections allow for people to actually have a voice.

Statement on Working with Consensus

My belief is that candidates should win on the strength and appeal of their policies, not by party. If an area is split even between parties no party should be winning more often than others simply because of the way the district is drawn. I have experience working on local political committees and I have experience with facilitation techniques. I also have a certificate for Collaborative Negotiations and Conflict Management for Environmental Professionals from the Northwest Environmental Training Center.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

At New College of Florida I did an undergraduate thesis where I analyzed the effects of mineral oil on several species of native plants. I am good at doing research and examining problems to find solutions that work best, not just the ones I want.
