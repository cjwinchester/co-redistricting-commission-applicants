Background

Professional Background

As a newspaper editor and publisher for over twenty years in SW Colorado, I developed a broad and deep understanding of issues in the region. As a candidate for the State House of Representatives for two consecutive cycles, I deeply engaged with voters on a wide range of issues.

Past Political Activity

I was a candidate for the Colorado State House of Representatives this year and two years ago. I have donated money to numerous candidates and several PACS.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

I have served on the board of the Telluride Library District, and currently serve on the board of the Telluride Mountain School and on the board of the Telluride Trust for Community Housing.

Statement

I strongly support the premise behind having an independent commission draw electoral districts. Districts should be drawn to make elections as fair as possible, without giving undue advantage to any party.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a newspaper editor, I learned that it is entirely possible to have a strong political point of view and at the same time to be impartial. I believe I exemplified this in my journalism, and I also observed it in years of attendance as a reporter covering government meetings. As a member of public and private boards, I have personally participated in building consensus and/or compromise to achieve results.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have written hundreds of editorials that work through thorny public issues.
