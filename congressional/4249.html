Statement

You need ethical, pragmatic, non-political eyes on an issue as critical to our democratic Republic as the redistricting process is. This process should have the highest priority of fair and equitable representation of the citizens of the state.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I am a very pragmatic and forward thinking person. I deal in facts and data by profession. I would first gain consensus on the desired outcome and work backwards on what conditions would have to exist in order for the desired outcome to be achieved. If feel if there is agreement on the outcome and all the conditions from the broadest to the most specific as a process, the potential for conflict is reduced and dealt with fact by fact, condition by condition. I feel this approach can avoid the larger, broad brush stroke arguments based on preferences, political alignments and biases and ideologies.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I must analyze data, identify trends, anticipate obstacles, impediments, risks in my work every day. I have extensive experience in large scale cross functional system integrations which require the ability to identify stakeholders, identify and rank risks involved from various viewpoints and foster and drive collaboration across functional teams. I must be able to see and understand the details of various systems and disciplines and stand back to the see the larger picture, implications and downstream impacts of decisions.
