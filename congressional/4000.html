Marvel & Colo State Grange, SW Repub. Women, La Plata County Fair Board, Hesperus Church. My involvement was greatly curtailed in 2011, when I quit many things to be able to care for my mother. She died in October, 2019.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I organized & represented La Plata County Republicans as their chair for 8 years. Represented our County in Colo. & National Assn. of Counties (served on NACO's public lands cmte). I often have advocated for rural residents of the county, state & nation. As County Comm., I needed to consider the interests of ALL citizens of our county (& sometimes the state).

Statement

The districts are important for fair representation of all citizens, perhaps most especially the rural &/or more conservative segments of our state. I am eager to make sure those citizens are considered & represented on the commission & in Congress.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a lifelong Coloradan, I am familiar with the state. As a one-time city dweller (Aurora & Durango), I understand that mindset; but as a "country girl" by upbringing, I know why rural residents sometimes feel under-represented & under-considered. I can bring that perspective to the commission, without being blind to the other viewpoints. Impartiality & fairness are part of my moral code & I have no personal stake in the outcome.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

In college, I majored in math, so my brain is wired to think logically. One of the most important questions to me is always, "Does it make sense?" Common sense is sometimes undervalued in relation to science & technology, I believe. I tend to question almost everything and will not accept something just because "someone" says it's so. It has to prove itself true & be sensible.
