Statement

I was an active supporter of this new commission and form of redistricting, reapportionment for our State as passed by the voters - with the hope that this would be a more fair and equitable process. I have testified on previous commissions when the decision has ended up in our Courts. As our State and in particular my County grows I would like to see less contrived Districts and additional representation that is needed to be drawn fairly.

My reputation is as a skilled, fair, hard working community builder. As my Resume' illustrates I have a great deal of experience building teams and consensus.
