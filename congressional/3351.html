Statement

I would like to serve for both altruistic and personal reasons. Having fair district boundaries is important for a healthy democracy. I do not believe it is better to have one set of voices in government. All geographical areas and diverse people should be represented. I am excited to see how this progresses as an independent commission and I wish to be a part of it. On a personal level I have found that I enjoy working with the public. I also have close to a hobby interest in learning about the constitution and how it has shaped this country.

Statement on Working with Consensus

Agreement is critical to success. Recognizing and addressing the needs of areas outside your own builds an environment of trust and engagement. My experience as a consultant and teacher has taught me to look to the needs of the organization without involving my own bias except for personal understanding. I work with many different clients and businesses with different goals. I volunteer with diverse populations from all over the world. It is my position to be curious about their needs and respond with understanding while still grounded in facts.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have many year experience as a data analyst and engineer. One strength is my ability to troubleshoot and find solutions where it may not seem possible. To do this I take steps to understand and describe the problem, ask questions and then logically try out ideas. Sometimes you get lucky and there is one solution. Most of the time you need to decide on the best solution.
