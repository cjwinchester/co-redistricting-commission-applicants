Background

Professional Background

When involved with the Classified Employee Council, I represented employees that experienced concerns and issues with regard to workers rights and safety issues. I headed the Adams State University Compensation committee that worked hard on an institutional policy that addressed salary adjustments of classified employees. I am also a member of Sacred Heart Church Social Action Committee, and volunteer for special celebrations. I am supportive of the needs of La Puente Home Shelter serving individuals experiencing homelessness, and providing community meals.

Past Political Activity

I have been an active member of the Alamosa County Democrats. As a former Colorado state employee, I have been committed to supporting worker-friendly candidates. I make small donations to local and regional candidates in House District 62 and volunteer to canvass and make calls for their campaigns.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Alamosa County Democrats. COWINS - Colorado Workers for Innovative and New Solutions. Adams State University LatinX Caucus. Adams State University Classified Employee Council. Adams State University Adams 100 Commission. Sacred Heart Catholic Church .

Statement

I feel by serving on the commission, I can share my perspective as a resident of the great San Luis Valley, in the south-central region of the state of Colorado. I want to serve my community and help support the concerns and needs of the people, the businesses and available services offered to the citizens of the San Luis Valley.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I can be a team player and would be respectful of the opinions of others. I would be open to listen to what others have to say. I will be fair, cooperative and represent my community and region with its best interest in mind.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

In my former position as a Customer Support Coordinator in the IT Department (at Adams State University), I conducted customer satisfaction and stakeholders' surveys, and analyzed the results. A comparison report was generated and shared with our department leads and provided us with a sense of how well we serve our customers and identify any concerns or issues that needed to be addressed.
