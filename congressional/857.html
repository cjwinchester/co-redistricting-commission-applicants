Statement

I view this redistricting effort as an opportunity to develop more equitable and competitive congressional districts that will provide a clearer reflection of the Colorado population in terms of our social, economic, racial and political diversity. Fairer representation will have the positive impact of countering justifiable cynicism resulting from historical gerrymandering and compensate for a compromised, perhaps distorted, census. A fair redistricting result, based on an open and defensible process, will help to restore belief in the integrity of our democratic processes as a whole. I would like to participate in this effort to clean up past districting biases and be part of Colorado's significant contribution to nationwide efforts to strengthen representative democracy in the face of a constricted census and continuing voter suppression activities.

Statement on Working with Consensus

The Commission as a whole could establish its ability to be fair and impartial and build consensus through the following logical sequence: 1. Define and commit to the specific process to be followed. 2. Define and commit to the criteria or framework to be used (e.g., equal size, maximizing competitiveness, maintaining minority communities and communities of interest). 3. Evaluate preliminary map in context of criteria. 4. Accurately summarize and evaluate community input. 5. Develop specific boundary options that may be more responsive to community concerns. 6. Identify and balance tradeoffs. 7. Develop defensible fact-based final position/recommendation consistent with criteria. I guarantee my personal ability to be fair and impartial. I am 100% committed to the idea of congressional representation being equitable and competitive. I am not acquainted with any potentially affected politicians and have not been involved in any congressional campaigns for over 10 years. I have no conceivable business or financial connections that could be impacted by congressional districts.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My entire body of work experience has centered on my analytical capabilities and being competent in: public administration, project management, proposal/report writing, project evaluation, analyzing impacts, budgeting, editing. Please see my attached resume.
