Statement

I saw a fake ad on facebook and wondered if it was real. I copied the ad to a reporter for CPR's twitter account and asked about it. One of her followers posted this link. Once I found out that the state was in fact taking applications the only thing left to do was decide if there was anyone I should recommend this job to. I believe in my ability to do this and I've hung around the house long enough. So instead of sending the link to an unemployed friend or family member I am asking for it myself.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I am neutral on most topics. Although I am not political as a volunteer at Church in the City I lead a discussion group of diverse people on Colfax. Asking questions is better than pushing my answer on others I find. In that group I had people who were in halfway houses or suffering from PTSD, addiction, loneliness and homelessness. Also I had people with no problems at all who worked as truckers, doctors, cops et al. It was interesting because no matter where they started once they came into the room they were on the same chapter of the same book.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I started programing in DOS as a child you have to be very careful because the computer will do what you tell it not what you want it to do. So my dad taught me how to make flow charts and decision trees. That way I would know what went wrong and why. As an I think in flow charts and explain everything in simple terms as if taking the other through a decision tree. If people spoken to in a calm logical way they come to an understanding of your view point even if they do not agree. After having an understanding I can see what view point is best. If mine is I will draw lines from the other persons toward mine. If they have the better view I'll be on their side and explain it to others who I think will join it.
