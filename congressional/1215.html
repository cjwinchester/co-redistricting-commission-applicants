Organization and Advocacy Experience

Serving in the Hickenlooper Administration in the Office of Community Partnerships & Special Initiatives, I helped oversee several statewide initiatives and act as a liaison between the State and our community partners and the communities they support. Our office was dedicated to listening and serving all Coloradoans equally and making a seat at the table for all those who wanted their voices to be heard. I understand the urban-rural divide in Colorado and the challenges it can present in terms of legislating and the importance of ensuring adequate and appropriate representation of all Colorado regions. Additionally, in my work as a social impact consultant, we work directly with communities to gather data to inform the strategy and approach of our work — doing field research, holding focus groups, and conducting interviews and surveys.

Statement

Redistricting will determine how we communicate and are represented by policymakers for the next 10 years. Right now, we have the opportunity to draw lines that will create a more equitable state by ensuring communities have the voices heard and needs addressed. I want to be part of that work and help drive the systemic change we need to create a system that works for everyone. Additionally, I represent a generation and community that has not often been part of these processes and can add valuable perspective and insight.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a student of Philosophy, I embrace the Socratic method and the process of meeting consensus. I am a pragmatic mediator who is committed to moving conversations forward rather than allowing gridlock and differing views to hinder progress. I listen just as much as I speak and value different perspectives, especially those very different from my own. Creating thoughtful and meaningful change is a long process that requires finding common ground and often compromise. I do not let my personal views get in the way of moving forward when the work is about serving everyone, not just those who align with my own. I value facts and evidence-based decisions and will do thorough research to inform my decisions — looking at a wide range of sources and checking the reliability of my sources. I am a data-driven and analytical thinker who can communicate finding those who do not have the same in-depth understanding of data and statistical models.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have a certification in data analytics and have taken several courses in data science. I believe the process of how we approach a problem and arrive at a conclusion determines the integrity and efficacy of the solution. I am a critical thinker who can diplomatically communicate and mediate differing opinions — clearly representing evidence and data to make a point.
