Organization and Advocacy Experience

As a public health professional, my career is rooted in advocating for Colorado's most vulnerable populations through the lens of social determinants of health. At CDPHE, I worked with rural, suburban and urban organizations. I championed for families on Medicaid and advocated for important services to protect young children and pregnant women. In my currently role, I advocate for accessible health information for all patients and families regardless of their background, education level or language.

I have spent a lot of my career and education learning about overt and implicit bias and I am constantly working on recognizing and addressing where my bias may be at play.
