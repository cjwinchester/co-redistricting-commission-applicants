Organization and Advocacy Experience

I have lived in Colorado for 39 years, and Nederland for 23. During that time I have spent time serving on (and in some cases creating) the boards of the majority of the non-profits of the Nederland area since I moved here, with an intention to strengthen and improve communication with these boards and collaboration between them. I also run the major Facebook community page for the town, have worked at the local newspaper, and served in roles in human services and disaster response.

Statement

I feel that I can provide a solid core of community spirit and belief in the mission of both human services, under-served populations and progressive political viewpoint to the process, to help keep it fair and equitable.

As a facilitation trainer and after 35 years on non-profit boards, I have cultivated and consistently practiced the art of collaboration and compromise. I am excellent at perceiving and understanding others' viewpoints, and finding common ground.

While I am not a scientist or mathematician, I have a good ability to understand complex arguments and understand subtleties. I scored extremely high on the LSAT when I took the test, although I did not choose to accept a position in a law school when it was offered.
