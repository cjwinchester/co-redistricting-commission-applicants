Statement

I believe strongly in fairness, sharing, and working for the betterment of all. To be honest, in the past I have avoided getting involved in politics because the partisanship I see is repugnant to me. My little experience when getting involved with Republicans was tainted by their focus on how much they hate Democrats and my experience when getting involved with Democrats was seeing how much they demonize Republicans. But this is a chance to work in a bipartisan way for fairness, reason, and good. I'm ready to dive in and work with both parties to make sure all voices in Colorado are heard.

Statement on Working with Consensus

The first order of business is to come to agreement on our mission. This provides both the foundation for our work and the guiding light for the direction we take at each junction and the way we resolve disputes. I'm an analytical by nature, but the thing my clients like best about me is the way I explain complicated things using terms they can understand. First I listen to learn their needs and concerns. Then I use facts and logic to come up with the optimal choice for each individual client and make sure they understand and agree. My ability to be fair and impartial is founded on my personal mission of replacing hatred and demonization in policitcs with fairness and understanding.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

For 20 years I did strategic marketing for Hewlett-Packard and then six start-ups. Working with R&D to prioritize features based on customer needs was the central part of my work. I am experienced in applying proven decision-making tools and have been a facilitator of brainstorming sessions, Ben Franklin's Advantages/Disadvantages process, decision trees, and more. These tools are applicable to the task at hand.
