Professional Background

Helping to contact individuals and businesses to participate in purchasing items or providing items for silent auction. I enjoy making contacts and organizing events. Organizing the group, finding performing opportunities, directing and performing with the group. Statement

The commission is providing a valuable role in creating the redistricting for the state, I would be honored to be a part of the process and know that I could be a balanced voice in determining the new districts. It is important that the state establish fair districts to represent all voices equally. I enjoy new learning opportunities, working and meeting new people. I feel I am a good collaborator from my years of teaching. I know how to be an effect listener and problem solver and know these traits would help me to be a contributing member of the redistricting team.

Statement on Working with Consensus

During my 33 years of teaching I had many opportunities to be involved in committees and meetings where we had to come to consensus. I served on the Education Association’s negotiations team. We had some very difficult decisions to make due to the recession and the district’s funds being reduced by the state. We were able to come up with creative ideas that helped teachers feel empowered and valued but take into consideration the difficulties the district was facing. I was very proud to be a part of that process. I feel I am a reasonable voice, willing to listen to many sides and form my own opinion, not the opinion expected of me by others. I like to participate with empathy and compassion. I do not feel that a person is defined by their political party and that people of all parties and backgrounds have much to offer and voices worth hearing.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

When analyzing an issue or a problem, I like to take a step back and look at it from the outside. Often it's easy to respond via emotion. I prefer to take a calm look at all the parameters and try to find several possible answers to the issue. It's also important to get feedback and input from other sources, to see how the possible solution will impact those closest to the issue.
