Statement

I got interested in redistricting issues after 2010 census, while Chicago Council was getting ready to draw new map of Chicago Wards. Attended redistricting hearings, testified on behalf of 36 Ward residents, consulted constitutional attorneys and promoted community input and awareness in local media, at neighborhood meetings and joined Fair Map group of activists. I believe that non-partisan approach to drawing the maps of our states could bring more fairness and diversity to local political districts. I am honest, competent, reliable and passionate about the subject.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have never been affiliated with any party in Colorado, and I always took into consideration many sides of controversial issues facing community or my children schools or the city I lived in. I am a good listener and I avoid judging people who have different views. I have respect for public servants and people, who donate their own time to make a difference in our lives. I know what "agree to disagree" statement means and I follow it in my public or private life.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

While working in real estate for almost 20 years, assisting property managers, clients and attorneys in zoning matters, I had to research zoning maps and regulations, advice in financial decisions and cooperate with local authorities to bring the best and compromising solutions to my clients/bosses problems.
