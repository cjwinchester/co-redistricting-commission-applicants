Statement

This is an important civic duty and I learned that women are underrepresented on both commissions. I am not currently employed or seeking paid employment, but I have been considering ways to make a difference during a challenging time. As a fairly new Coloradan, I want an opportunity to contribute to making this state an even better place to live. As an American, I can give back to my country by helping to make this effort a successful example for others. The future of our democracy depends on bipartisan and nonpartisan efforts to ensure our system is fair and that voters have confidence in that system.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I was a leader in the Air Force and the Air Force Reserve for 27 years, dealing with contentious issues and brokering agreement among multiple stake holders constantly. I’m a good listener who asks a lot of questions and seeks to understand motivations without judging those motivations. People care about what they care about and I encourage everyone to be open and honest, which fosters an atmosphere of fairness. Most situations are not a zero sum game and I strive to ensure other people see that. I am usually successful in helping others reframe their thinking about issues and/or seeing how narrow issues fit into a larger picture or higher purpose.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I was an Intelligence Officer, so critical thinking, analysis, and helping decision makers understand complex problems and threats was my whole job. I was involved in numerous efforts to define new threats, which requires creating analytical frameworks and methodologies. If you have seen the new Colorado COVID dial dashboard - that is a good example of the kinds of projects I worked on - defining threat levels and corresponding risk assessments/mitigation measures. From 9/11 onward, I led teams analyzing terrorism and terrorist networks in Europe, and most recently, I was assigned to US Strategic Command, where I led large teams (30+) during nuclear weapons employment exercises requiring analysis of Russia’s and China’s nuclear weapons and space programs.
