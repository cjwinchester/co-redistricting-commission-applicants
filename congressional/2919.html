Organization and Advocacy Experience

As a municipal clerk, I facilitate the operation of 9 different Boards and Commissions appointed by the Westminster City Council, including recruitment, interviewing, appointing, on-boarding, and continuing education of members; as well as ongoing support of each board's activities and public meetings. As a liaison to the City Council, I also serve the public directly in matters related to municipal elections, legislative actions and policy decisions of the Council, issuance of liquor licenses and business licenses; public records requests and fulfillment, and custody of city records.

Statement

I wish to contribute in a meaningful way to changing the way that representative districts are drawn.

Statement on Working with Consensus

For the vast majority of my professional life, it has been my job to act fairly and in an impartial manner. As an elections administrator, working together with politically opposed parties to coordinate on free and fair elections for the voters of my county was a huge part of my job. As a City Clerk, I must also work with a board of elected officials in a completely impartial manner, to ensure that staff is carrying out their policy decisions and legislative actions. As a leader and manager, I have years of experience in finding ways to address conflicts and find common ground.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My entire career has been dependent on my ability to analyze and interpret various local, state, and federal laws as they relate to elections administration or municipal operations. I have been the lead on many mission-critical, complex projects, and I spent several years as an election administrator focusing on technical writing of election law procedures manuals for the field. In addition, I have been part of a high-level team developing custom software programs and elections systems, and in my current position I have implemented many processes to streamline and improve efficiency in public administration.
