Statement

I wish to serve on the Commission because I believe that we desperately need, as a nation, to change our districting so that the voice and vote of minority citizens and residents are no longer marginalized or sniffled altogether; and to root out control by minority voting blocks that are not representative to larger society interests and needs.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As a professional neutral, I am regularly called upon to exercise fairness and impartiality, and also to be aware of and control for my implicit biases, and to note those biases in others where relevant to the decision making process. As a highly trained and professional dispute resolution professional (including approximately 90+ hours of formal mediation training and untold meditations and public facilitations in the past), I am pretty good at working with & identifying areas of accord between groups of individuals from different backgrounds, a/o who are in a situation of conflict, or who do not always see eye-to-eye.
