Statement

I like working on processes and with diverse groups of people. I am not very active in my political party and I am involved in various groups within my professional community that allows consensus and many voices to be heard. I think this commission is an opportunity to use my organizational skills and passion for including all voices to impact CO in a positive way.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I currently work on a consensus group for an ANSI standard and in that capacity work with users, vendors and general interest groups to ensure a safety standard meets the needs of all. I have also been a stakeholder and staff that has contributed to draft Regulations language in CO, which requires cooperation. As someone not super involved in political work I think I can be fair and impartial by keeping an open mind and recognizing that creating maps that include all voices is ultimately in the best interest of the state and myself and my neighbors.
