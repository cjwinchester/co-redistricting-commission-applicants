Statement

I want to serve on the commission because I want to be part of the solution to fix issues in unfair districting. Partisan gerrymandering is wrong. Participating in the independent Colorado commissions for redistricting is an exciting and leading-edge opportunity to avoid the unfairness that has been so prevalent in this country's history. Recent political events have driven me to get more involved with the processes that ultimately affect our very way of life. I truly want to do what I can to make sure that the people are heard and to try to make things better for all of us in Colorado, and in the USA.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I will bring enthusiasm to fair redistricting principles, using math and logic. I would point out any discrepancies in any proposed redistricting that does not follow principles of fair and equitable representation. Working together as a team, we can brainstorm to ensure everyone's ideas are heard, then work through the ideas to vet those that may be able to be applied and eliminate any that will not work toward equitable and fair districting. I strive to see all sides of a problem and both sides of an argument. I am a fan of writing down all of the viewpoints, comparing them, and taking the best ideas from each side to collaborate and arrive at an agreeable solution.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My entire career has been dedicated to applying principles of logic and reason using analytical skills in a step-by-step manner. I use structured methods to define a problem, develop solutions, evaluate those potential solutions, and ultimately come to a consensus on the optimal solution. This allows the facts and "givens" to be understood up front, first by doing research/information & data gathering to define what is known and using the results to develop possible solutions. Next, the pros & cons/strengths and weaknesses of each proposed solution must be evaluated and the implications must be understood. Finally, articulating the ideas and information and sharing with other members of the commission for constructive discussion and collaboration to find an optimal, feasible solution.
