Statement

I have a passion for engaging 18-30 years in the political system and serve as an example that we can create real change in our communities. I am incredibly passionate about fairness and justice and want to lend my skills to a commission that is facilitating this goal. I think that my role as a Mountain Corridor community member can provide valuable insight into the needs of these communities that may often get missed in the Front Range. I can provide valuable experience in facilitating difficult discussions in a healthy manner.

Statement on Working with Consensus

As an individual that grew up staunchly Republican transitioned into an Independent and then became a Democrat later in life, I pride myself on the ability to rationalize and understand each side. I have a deep understanding of policy understandings on both sides of the aisle and pride myself on the ability to find some consensus between each of the parties. One of my favorite political questions to ask is: What about your own side gives you pause? What about the other side do you appreciate? I would take this sentiment into the commission by encouraging members to reframe the issue and their understanding along these lines.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

My long background in communications ensures that I can take a difficult and nuanced problem and be able to parse out the relevant information and find a method of reaching a conclusion.
