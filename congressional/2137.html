Background

Past Political Activity

Currently Chair for the Broomfield County Democrats (since February 2017), volunteered and donated to Bernie Sanders, Hillary Clinton and other Democratic candidates in 2016 election, volunteered and donated to Democratic candidates in 2018 election, volunteered and donated to Democratic candidates in 2020 election. Prior to 2015 maintained nonpartisan positions as required by the Hatch Act.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

One Broomfield Indivisible since January 2017, member of ACLU & League of Women Voters.

Organization and Advocacy Experience

I am currently the Chair for the Broomfield County Democrats and lead this organization in organizing, representing, and advocating for community interests in local, regional, state, and national political efforts.

Statement

Colorado is leading the way in assuring that the democratic principles of representational government are real and reflected demographically and geographically in the mechanics of our politics. Colorado embodies the ideals which can provide a model for national changes to assure that balance is achieved in our political institutions. I am a minority female who brings with me experience in working with a variety of individuals to pursue shared interests as a representative of management, labor, partisan and nonpartisan organizations.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe that balance is essential to successful governance and bring a value system that respects differing views. I was raised in northern New Mexico in a small rural community and have lived in the Denver metro area since 1979. I worked in a variety of Federal agencies including the National Weather Service, the Internal Revenue Service, and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency. I have worked with a range of communities including Indian Country, rural communities, and urban areas. I have been successful and demonstrated an ability to work through conflicting interests and identify solutions.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I am a strategic thinker with demonstrated organizational and process skills honed through leadership of volunteer organizations. My decision-making process includes gathering information and listening to stakeholders. The goals of this Commission are foundational and will rely upon complete, reliable, and defensible quantitative and qualitative data. My experience includes evaluating the scientific basis for environmental regulation from a non-scientist perspective. A significant portion of my success has been due to my ability to communicate with the general public about how scientific data relates to and supports regulatory parameters.
