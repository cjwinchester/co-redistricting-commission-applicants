Statement

Our Republic functions and thrives when our elective representatives effectively represent the will of the people. Over the last twenty years, I have observed hyper partisan gerrymandering being utilized to disenfranchise one group or another. The redistricting process has been hijacked by both parties to achieve shortsighted political objectives in the legislature. What I admire about the non-partisan redistricting commission is end this shortsighted political gerrymandering that undermines the confidence in our democracy. I envision a commission that leverages data to redistrict in a fashion that is fair and just for all and that provides each of the 64 counties appropriate elective representation and voice for all Coloradans in the public policy process.
