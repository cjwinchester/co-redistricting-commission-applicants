Statement

EVERY citizen deserves a vote that counts. I have lived in redlined and gerrymandered districts where dissenting opinions and views were totally ignored due to how district lines were drawn. Districts should be drawn on the basis of population, not partisan interests. As a Councilperson and a strategic planner I have used GIS and other tools to draw and layer districts to give the greatest voice to the most people.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have been in positions of leadership since my youth. I learned one key to consensus building to to drill down past positions and positioning to underlying wants, needs and values. It is at that level there is more agreement that difference.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I started programming computers at 16, and then worked as a trader and broker analyzing companies for investment. As a small business and nonprofit consultant spent nearly 30 years helping solve problems and removing obstacles to success. It seems my entire life has been resolving problems.
