Statement on Working with Consensus

I am an natural born leader, but I also know how to listen to others views, I manage projects as a full time job, which includes interacting with lots of different opinions and personality types. I am also a middle child to 2 sisters and this prepared me to be a mediator for all of life! I want to represent the best interests of ALL Coloradan's. I grew up in Kansas in a conservative, Republican leaning household. When I moved to Colorado in 2001, I was unaffiliated for many years because I didn't see myself as aligning with one particular political party. As I have gotten older (and because I wanted to vote in the Primary election), I changed my affiliation to Democrat. I still feel like I have moderate views in many ways, but error on the side of wanting to make sure our most vulnerable populations are taken care of too.
