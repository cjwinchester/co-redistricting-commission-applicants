Statement

I am very interested in contributing to the work of the commission and getting this project done equitably and smoothly. I truly enjoy project management and strategic planning, and this commission fits right in with those interested. I am a good fit because I am moderate and empathic to needs on both sides of the aisle. I will strive to be respectful and inclusive and equity-driven in all my actions as a commissioner.
