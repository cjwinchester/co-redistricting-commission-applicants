Background

Professional Background

In colorado springs another woman and I began PPA future which was a group of concerned citizens rallying to get city council to create oil and gas regulations for the city and county. We held meetings monthly, had a website and facebook page and had speakers like Josh Fox and Wes Wilson come speak to our members and to city council. I was a member of OW attending GA and helping with our social media out reach. For just myself I started the 'No fracking in Colorado Springs' Page on FB and used it to get information out about upcoming events and situations across the country and world that had to do with oil and gas.

Past Political Activity

I was involved with Occupy Wallstreet in 2011-2013 at which point I began attending city council meetings in Colorado Springs in regards to oil and gas regulations and MJ legalization and sales. I have donated to the Progressive Democrats of America in regards to getting Bernie Sanders to run. I got involved in Bernie Sanders campaign as soon as he announced and have been donating to him in small increments since. I have also donated to various races across the country that were tied to his policy proposals.

List of Political and Civic Organizations belonged to

Prior to 11/2015 I was involved in a few organizations but since then I have basically been a PCP for my district servicing 2 terms.

Statement

Redistricting is such a very important aspect of our democracy that most citizens don't even know about. When I heard on NPR that this commission was being put together I shared it on my social media and made sure to try to get out the word. I really feel like keeping a district fair and balanced can seriously shape how our democracy looks.

Statement on Working with Consensus

While I admit that I am very progressive because of that I feel like it is important to include everyone in how we behave in regards to voting and the ability to open avenues for it to everyone- regardless of their own view points. I can and do look at both sides of every issue before I make my own decisions. I care more about the collective than I do about just myself. It isn't always an easy position to be in but I feel that we all pledge to a social contract when we are part of a society and sometimes that means letting my own desires put aside in order to help others. In this I believe I would be an asset to a larger group.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I have always been a person who can look at different sides to a problem or question and find weaknesses and strengths within it. While being very outgoing I can take time to sit back and listen to someone else's perspective and add that to my own decisions about a problem to help find a solution that works for the most instead of the few.
