Statement

I have a very strong desire bring our political process more to an inclusive, fairer process. Seeing over the years what money and narrow partisan interests have created a system where people do not feel represented and are less trusting of the process. If I could, in some small way strengthen the process I would be most pleased.

Statement on Working with Consensus

The dialogue is most important. It will require strength, dedication and a sense of fairness. We need to again look at "the greater good" not always toward our own self interest.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I feel I have the ability to understand all sides. I am a consensus builder and people find me easy to talk to and are comfortable that I am not judgmental. I have a strong sense of community and want Colorado to be better. We need healing so badly. I feel I would be an asset to the process.
