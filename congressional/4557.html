Statement

I have been interested in politics and their impact on our lives since I was a teenager. I am particularly passionate about ensuring that redistricting is done in such a way that it gives an accurate and effective voice to as many people as possible.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I have served, and continue to serve, on a number of different boards and committees, in addition to my role as CFO for a non-profit community centered board. My leadership style has always been to focus on the desired outcome, make sure everyone feels heard, and ask leading questions in order to arrive at agreement and consensus. My training as a CPA, along with my natural inclination to analysis gives me the ability to evaluate situations and projects impartially, based on facts rather than emotions.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

As a CPA and MBA, my training, as well as my role as a CFO consist primarily of analyzing data and situations, and applying logic and reason to problems to come up with the best solution.
