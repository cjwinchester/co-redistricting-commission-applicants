Statement

I have seen what political gerrymandering of redistricting efforts have done in the past. I wish to see districts that are competitive where ideas and issues get debated in the public sphere and am hopeful that with greater competitiveness comes greater accountability to constituents and the general public.

Statement on Working with Consensus

I believe in bridging the divides that separate us in our politics and worldviews right now. I believe there are productive ways forward that can be found using not a winner-take-all model of decision-making, but rather building consensus through shared listening and work. Even though I am a Democrat, I believe I can be fair and impartial when it comes to drawing the lines of those who will represent us in Washington, DC. I feel like both Democrats and Republicans in the past have used redistricting to solidify and consolidate power and I hope to be a part of the solution. That solution, to me, is making redistricting less about politics/power and more about representation.

Relevant Analytic Skills for Commission

I'm a critical thinker who is able to digest complex problems in a way that finds simple solutions in the midst of the details. I work well in face-paced, team environments in which a common goal is set before us. I have worked on campaigns that analyze voter data in a way that identifies and targets individuals for unique messaging asks. This experience and my attention to detail make me unique among candidates.
